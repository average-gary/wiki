# Initiative Tracker (Obsidian)

## Features
- Add/remove creatures with name, HP, AC, init.
- Auto-sort by initiative.
- XP calc by level/CR.
- Group identical creatures (e.g. 4× goblins).
- Multiple encounters in one code-block; auto save/load encounter state.

## Fantasy Statblocks bridge
"Supports both custom and SRD creatures from the Fantasy Statblocks plugin" — drop a creature reference, get its full block + numbers in the tracker.

## PF2e support
Not explicitly documented on the landing page. PF2e's initiative-as-Perception, three-action economy, and condition tracking are the things any PF2e-targeted tool must natively model — Initiative Tracker is system-agnostic and won't model conditions/actions out of the box.

## Sibling plugins (same author)
- **Obsidian Leaflet** — interactive maps in markdown.
- **Dice Roller** — inline dice mechanics.
- Together with Fantasy Statblocks, this is the canonical Obsidian-TTRPG stack.

## Relevance
Reinforces that Obsidian's plugin ecosystem covers wiki + statblocks + initiative + maps + dice — the "free / DIY" stack a paid PF2e tool must outperform on PF2e-specific automation (action economy, conditions, MAP, encounter budget).
