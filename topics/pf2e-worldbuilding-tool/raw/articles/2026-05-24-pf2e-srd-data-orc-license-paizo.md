# ORC License — Key Extracts

## Registration & Stewardship

- **Library of Congress registration**: TX 9-307-067 (the canonical, immutable text)
- **Drafted by**: Azora Law (with input from "hundreds of independent publishers")
- **Owner**: No RPG publisher owns it; deliberately structured without a controlling entity. Paizo *publishes* it but does not *control* it.
- **Released**: July 2023
- **Companion document**: ORC AxE (Answers & Explanations) — official FAQ filed under the same copyright registration.

## Structure

- **Section I: Definitions** — Licensed Material, Reserved Material, Derivative Work, etc.
- **Section II: Grants & Limitations**
- **Section III: Required Notice** — attribution
- **Section IV: Warranty & Limitation of Liability**
- **Section V: Other Terms & Conditions** — termination, modification, construction

## Licensed Material (functional, freely usable)

- Character creation systems: stats, attributes, classes, abilities
- Gameplay mechanics: combat, initiative, skill checks, resource management
- Methods for determining success/failure
- Play mediums: dice rolling, character sheets, tokens

## Reserved Material (NOT licensed; stays with original publisher)

- Trademarks and trade dress
- Creative expressions: visual art, music, characters, settings, storylines
- Proper nouns and derived names

## Grant Language

> "Worldwide, royalty-free, non-sublicensable, non-exclusive, irrevocable license" to use Licensed Material.

## Obligations on Downstream Users

1. **Attribution Notice**: Credit all upstream licensors
2. **ORC Notice**: Standardized notice identifying license location at LoC TX 9-307-067
3. **Reserved Material Disclosure**: Identify any Reserved Material being retained
4. **Downstream Licensing**: Grant recipients the same ORC rights (copyleft-like for mechanics)

## Why It Matters

- **Irrevocable**: Once released under ORC, content cannot be withdrawn — protects against the OGL 1.1 scenario.
- **Perpetual**: Lasts copyright term.
- **Unmodifiable**: License text itself cannot be amended by any party.
- **System-agnostic**: Not tied to PF2e specifically; usable for any RPG.

## Implications for a PF2e Worldbuilding/LLM Tool

A tool that ships PF2e mechanical data can do so under ORC if the upstream source is ORC-licensed (Player Core, GM Core, Monster Core, Player Core 2, and post-2023 Paizo publications). The tool MUST include the ORC Notice, the upstream attribution chain, and clearly distinguish its own content from Reserved Material (e.g., proper nouns like "Golarion," "Cheliax," deity names tied to Paizo's setting are Reserved unless separately licensed via Community Use or Pathfinder Infinite).
