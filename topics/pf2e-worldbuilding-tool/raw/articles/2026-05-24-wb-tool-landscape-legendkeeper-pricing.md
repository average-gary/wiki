# LegendKeeper Pricing & Features

## Tiers

| Tier | Price | Capability |
|------|-------|------------|
| Basic | Free | View & export projects only; collaborate as guest |
| Pro | $9/mo or $90/yr (~$7.50/mo annual; 16% off) | Full creator features; 14-day trial, no card |

## Pro Features (all "unlimited")
- Wiki pages, maps & asset sizes
- Timelines and events
- Whiteboards (infinite canvas)
- Collaborators (only project owner needs subscription — guests are free)
- Storage
- Publish to web

## Notable positioning quotes
- "You own your data, not us" — emphasizes export integrity even after subscription lapses.
- Collaborator-friendly licensing: ownership-only sub means you can run a campaign as the sole paying GM with player editors free.

## Mobile / offline
- Offline-first with sync on reconnect.
- No native mobile yet; mobile-web optimization in progress, native apps "planned later."

## Key gaps
- No mention of AI features.
- No system-specific (D&D / PF2e) integrations.
- No price tiers — flat $9/mo means casual users can't pay less; power users can't unlock more.

## Relevance
Demonstrates the "modern flat-pricing wiki" model competing with World Anvil's tier sprawl; sets a $90/yr ceiling a PF2e-native tool must beat or differentiate from clearly.
