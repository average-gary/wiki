# CoinDesk — "Plebs Eat First": Parasite Finds Second Block

Independent third-party validation of Parasite Pool operating in production.

## Empirical record

- **Block #938,713** — late February 2026 (first mainnet block; famously found by a miner renting hashrate from **Braiins Hashpower** for ~$200 and pointing it at Parasite, taking the 3.125 BTC).
- **Block #945,601** — April 18, 2026. ~48 days after the first block.
- Hashrate **~52 PH/s** at second block (down from June-2025 peak of 182 PH/s; ~0.005% of network).

## Reaffirms

- 1 BTC to finder + remainder shared
- 0% fee
- Targeted at home miners / Bitaxe-class hardware

## Why ingestion-worthy

Establishes empirical operating record and timestamps. Validates economic survivability across long inter-block gaps, pre-launch claims, and confirms that the scheme runs in production. Pairs with founder-side narrative as the independent corroboration.
