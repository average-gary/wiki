## Project site (hashpool.dev)

States the project is **"in active development"** and that **testnet instances are running on testnet4**.

Explicit warning: *"this is a test instance and the protocol is still under heavy development"*. **No mainnet readiness signal.**

User-facing pitch:
- No minimum withdrawal threshold
- Anonymous (no-KYC) mining
- Tokenized "eHash" shares accruing toward block rewards

Acknowledges custodial trust assumption: *"DO NOT store your life savings in an ecash mint"* — *"only keep enough… to cover daily or monthly expenses."*
