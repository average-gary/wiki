# A New Mining Pool Emerges — Blockspace Media

Industry newsletter piece authored by zk-shark himself, in technical voice. Closest thing to a primary-source spec document for Parasite Pool.

## Confirmed mechanics

- 1 BTC finder bonus + cumulative-shares-since-last-block residual (no rolling N-window).
- Lightning fanout via "coinbase alchemy" — pool fronts liquidity to settle before the 100-block coinbase maturity.
- Zero pool fee; 10-sat minimum payout.

## Comparable cottage pools listed

- ckpool (solo)
- solopool
- OCEAN

The framing situates Parasite as a **fourth design lineage** distinct from FPPS, classical PPLNS, and solo. Author claims the scheme "warrants a new terminology."
