# Ark Labs $5.2M Tether-Led Raise (Mar 2026)

The **other** Ark camp. Establishes the two-camp landscape: Second.tech (Roose) vs Ark Labs/Arkade (Burak's lineage).

## Funding facts

- **$5.2M seed** announced March 2026.
- Cumulative **$7.7M** with prior $2.5M pre-seed (August 2024).
- **Lead**: Tether.
- **Participants**: Ego Death Capital, Epoch VC, Lion26, Sats Ventures, Contribution Capital, Anchorage Digital, Ralph Ho (ex-PayPal).
- **Pre-seed (Aug 2024)**: Draper Associates, Fulgur Ventures, Axiom Capital.

## Positioning — NOT mining

- Pitch: **stablecoins / programmable finance on Bitcoin**, not mining.
- Tether's Paolo Ardoino: "Stablecoins were born on Bitcoin… Ark Labs is building infrastructure that makes it easier to issue, move, and settle stablecoins directly on Bitcoin."
- Arkade product launched **October 2025** ("Press Start: Arkade Goes Live", 2025-10-20).
- Repos: `arkade-os/wallet` (Oct 2024), `arkade-os/rust-sdk` + `arkade-os/ts-sdk` (Dec 2024).
- BTCPayServer plugin (Nov 2025).
