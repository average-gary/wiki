# DMND Launch — Bitcoin Magazine (Mar 2025)

Documents DMND's commercial frame at launch.

## Findings

- **Operator entity**: Guru Protocol Ltd (UK)
- **Lead VC**: Trammell Venture Partners (Christopher Calicott); amount undisclosed
- **Founding-miner economics**: 0% pool fee for first two months
- **Two-year founding-miner agreement** — non-trivial lock-in for early adopters
- **Standard fee % not disclosed** in this announcement
