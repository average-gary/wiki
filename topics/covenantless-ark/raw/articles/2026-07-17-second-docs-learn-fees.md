# Ark fees (Second/Bark docs)

Part of the [[2026-07-17-second-tech-docs-learn-manifest.md|Second Learn-section collection]].

- **Fee categories** (no amounts on this page): liquidity costs (capital opportunity cost for refreshes/Lightning/on-chain), on-chain Bitcoin fees, Lightning routing fees, development costs, server operating costs.
- "any Ark server **must** charge fees (in some way or another) to cover their operational costs."
- **Two pricing models**: (1) **direct cost-based** — "each operation is priced based on its actual cost to the server"; (2) **abstracted** — cross-subsidization for "more predictable pricing." Second uses a **hybrid**.
- Concrete fee schedule/amounts are on Second's pricing page, not in the docs.
