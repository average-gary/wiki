# TripleA — triplea-game/triplea

Java/GPL-3.0 turn-based strategy / board-game engine. *"Similar to Axis &
Allies or Risk."*

## Stack

- **Java 99.8%**, **GPL-3.0**
- Active **lobby system** for live multiplayer
- Community-created maps span WWII, historical, and fantasy scenarios

- **Very active** — comparable to Unciv's release cadence

## Scope note (important)

TripleA is **not 4X**. It is a **tactical / board-game engine** — Axis &
Allies–style. It belongs in the wiki only as an "adjacent / out-of-scope"
boundary marker.

The wiki should document **why TripleA is excluded**:
- No exploration / expansion / tech-tree growth phase
- Players play pre-defined scenarios; no procedural map gen
- Combat is the core loop; economy is thin

This boundary is itself useful — it pins down what "civ-like" actually means.

## Why ingest anyway

A short stub article can pin TripleA as the canonical "active OSS strategy
project that is *not* a civ-like" reference. Helps the wiki taxonomy stay
crisp.
