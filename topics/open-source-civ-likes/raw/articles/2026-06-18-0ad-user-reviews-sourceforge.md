# 0 A.D. user reviews (SourceForge)

User-submitted reviews on SourceForge. Concrete user-level complaints — gives
texture beyond "OSS dev is slow."

## Concrete complaints

- **Performance**: *"the high demand this game puts on the hardware makes
  it completely useless - unplayable"* — common complaint despite the
  game's pre-2010-era visual style.
- **AI imbalance**: *"unfair due to the amount of warriors that come at
  once"* — overwhelming rushes rather than nuanced play.
- **UI overhead**: *"You spend more time on building, troop, and resource
  management than actually warring."*
- **Mac compatibility broken**: cursor selection bugs reported on MacBook
  Pro (*"can't do anything once the game starts"*).
- **Missing tutorials** — onboarding is a known weakness, repeatedly cited.

## Why this matters

User-grade complaints from the field, complementing the structural critique
(long alpha cycle). The persistent perf / AI-rush / Mac / tutorial gaps
are useful product-management input for any future contributor or wiki
reader weighing the project.
