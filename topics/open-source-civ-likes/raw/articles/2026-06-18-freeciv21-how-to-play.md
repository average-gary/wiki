# How to Play Freeciv21 (Official Manual)

Single-player-focused gameplay primer. Hands off install to a separate
"Installing Freeciv21" page and online play to the "Longturn Game Playing
Guide."

## Concrete UI paths surfaced

- `Civilization > Government > Revolution`
- `Game > Load Another Tileset`
- `Unit > Fortify/Sentry`
- `Work > Build City`
- `Help > Terrain > Terrain Alterations`

## Core mechanics covered

- City placement: **Smallpoxing** (many small cities) vs. **Largepoxing**
  (few large cities) tradeoff
- **Settlers / Workers / Warriors** build order
- Worker land improvement
- Government changes; tech research; diplomacy; military

## Onboarding stance

The manual acknowledges the learning curve and **recommends complete
newcomers "start by reading the legacy Freeciv Manual" first** — Freeciv21
docs intentionally lean on upstream Freeciv docs for foundational concepts.

## Game-design quote

> *"The idea is to balance the quality of the site you find against getting
> your first city established as early as possible. You will find that
> balance becomes a key aspect of playing Freeciv21."*

This is the canonical first-stop for a new Freeciv21 player.
