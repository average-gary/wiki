## [2026-06-15] research | "fedimint reproducible builds" → +4 sources, +2 articles, +1 thesis
Round 3 (5 standard agents). Headline: Fedimint has the **strongest build-time tooling** in Bitcoin-adjacent Rust (Flakebox + crane 3-stage caching + fenix toolchain pin + dpc's `replaceGitHash`/`bbe` pattern + 5 cross-compile targets) and the **weakest verification cohort** (n=1 manual-spot-check per @dpc in issue #4305; PR #4339 ships sign-release without a public-key roster; Cachix as TOFU acknowledged in issue #2149). Two-tier gap below Bitcoin Core's `guix.sigs` (16-23 attesters), one-tier below LND (5 signers). Federation-trust-paradox: Byzantine-tolerant consensus + single-builder-TOFU supply chain. Fedi's production iOS/Android wallet closed-source (mobile gap unfixable without open-sourcing). New: concept `rust-nix-build-stack`, topic `fedimint-reproducible-builds`, thesis candidate `fedimint-needs-fedimint-sigs`.
