Pulpit AI publishes three monthly tiers: Basic $39/mo (5 file uploads/mo), Standard $59/mo (10 uploads/mo, marked "most popular"), Pro $129/mo (25 uploads/mo). Annual plans give ~10% off ($421.20, $637.20, $1,393.20 respectively). Free trial: 2 free uploads, no credit card. All tiers include unlimited custom and automatic clips, captions, sermon summaries, discussion guides, devotionals, blog posts, newsletters, and unlimited chat with sermon content. Notable: Pulpit's pricing is roughly 4–10× Logos AI's per-month tier, indicating sermon repurposing tools command a premium over Bible-study assistants — the buyer (pastor with church budget) is different from the lay-study buyer.
