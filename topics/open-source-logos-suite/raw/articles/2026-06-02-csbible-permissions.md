Holman Bible Publishers' permissions page for the Christian Standard Bible. Terms mirror NASB/NLT shape: up to 1,000 verses quotable without written permission; the quoted text cannot exceed 50% of the host work; cannot quote a complete book; required CSB attribution boilerplate including the trademark designation. The page contains NO mention of an API, electronic-distribution program, or developer license. Holman/Lifeway has no published self-serve developer API for CSB as of 2026-06-02. Anyone wanting CSB programmatic access must contact Holman/B&H permissions directly. CSB on api.bible (American Bible Society) appears under unique-license terms — not generally accessible through the standard api.bible developer key.
