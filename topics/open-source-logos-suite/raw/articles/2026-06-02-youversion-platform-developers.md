YouVersion's developer platform (developers.youversion.com / platform.youversion.com). Markets "Free Bible APIs" but access is partner-gated, not public self-serve. Claims "Licensed and official access to more than a thousand Bible versions in many languages, including the NIV." Onboarding flows through platform.youversion.com/partners — applicants must apply to become an authorized partner, terms negotiated case-by-case. Available endpoints include text, audio, and reading-plan content; iOS/Android/web SDKs are part of the offer. Key fact for OSS-app planning: even a "free" API tier here is conditioned on partnership approval, which means YouVersion can decline applicants on undisclosed grounds and there is no public revocation-event database. YouVersion is the dominant mobile Bible app (>500M installs) and has historically been the largest single licensee for many publishers — but that leverage flows the other way for downstream developers, who are at the discretion of YV's partner team.
