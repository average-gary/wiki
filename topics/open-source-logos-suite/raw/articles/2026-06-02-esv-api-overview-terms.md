The Crossway ESV API public-facing terms page (esv.org/api). Confirms still-active 2026 rate limits — 5,000 queries/day, 1,000/hour, 60/minute — and the 500-verses-or-half-a-book ceiling that applies to (a) per-query response, (b) per-page render, and (c) local cache. Reaffirms a non-commercial-only restriction (no fees, no advertising, no sponsorships). Quotes the doctrinal-conformity clause requiring affirmation of the historic Christian understanding of inspiration, Trinity, deity/death/resurrection of Christ, and salvation by grace alone in Christ. Quotes the revocation language: "We reserve the right to cancel your access to the service at any time for any reason" and access keys may be revoked for any term breach. Required attribution: ESV copyright notice and a link to esv.org on every page.
