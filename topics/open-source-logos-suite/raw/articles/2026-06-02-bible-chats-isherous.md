Cross-platform Bible-RAG demo: Flutter UI, Firebase/Firestore backend, Google Gemini for generation, Pinecone for vector retrieval, LangChain (Python) for orchestration. 6 stars, updated July 2025. Web demo at bible-chats.web.app. The author explicitly notes the Bible was chosen as a widely-recognized reference text (not a religious endorsement), which speaks to the app's framing as a generic semantic-Q&A demo. License not stated. Pricing: free demo with developer absorbing Gemini/Pinecone/Firebase costs. Architectural lesson: this is the SaaS-default shape (proprietary cloud LLM + proprietary vector DB) — exactly the shape christ-is-lord should reject in favor of local-first / BYO-model.
