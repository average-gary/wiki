The official AndBible (open-source Android Bible study app, GPL-3.0) FAQ. Contains a dedicated entry "I can't find ESV any more in Downloads. What's wrong?" that states verbatim: "The publishers of the ESV have unfortunately decided it is no longer in their interest to publish towards the SWORD platform (by Crosswire Bible Society) which AndBible uses." No date is given. The FAQ also clarifies the AndBible distribution model: "All documents available in AndBible are either in the public domain or licensed for distribution" — sources are Crosswire, IBT Russia, eBible, STEPBible, and AndBible itself. This is documented evidence of a real walled-translation withdrawal event affecting downstream OSS Bible apps. Existing users can backup/restore the previously-downloaded module but new users cannot acquire it.
