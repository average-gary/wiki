Crossway's 10-point doctrinal statement, which is the substantive content the ESV API doctrinal-conformity clause points to. Notable points: (1) Bible as inspired and inerrant in original autographs, 66 books; (2) Trinity — one God in three persons; (3) Christ fully God and fully Man, substitutionary atonement; (4) salvation by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone; (5) Holy Spirit indwelling believers; (6) church as the body of all true believers; (7) two ordinances — baptism and Lord's Supper; (8) eternal security of every true child of God; (9) bodily resurrection of saved and lost; (10) eternal communion with God. This is unambiguously a conservative/Reformed-evangelical confession; it would screen out e.g. Latter-Day Saint, Jehovah's Witness, Christian Universalist, Process Theology, and overtly liberal/non-creedal positions on the doctrines listed in the API clause.
