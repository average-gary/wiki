The official Logos AI page describes "AI-powered search and summarization" available across all subscription tiers and "AI translation" on the Max tier. Pricing is presented as Premium $8.33/mo (annual; ~$9.99/mo monthly), Pro $12.50/mo (annual), Max $16.67/mo (annual), all with a 60-day free trial. Premium ships ~200 curated books, Pro ~400 (sermon prep, Greek/Hebrew), Max ~600 (advanced language tools, AI translation). Critically, the page does **not** disclose the underlying RAG architecture, which LLM(s) it calls, whether retrieval is scoped to the user's licensed library, the citation format, or any hallucination-mitigation strategy. This opacity is itself a finding: incumbent vendors are not publishing how their AI grounds its answers, leaving users to trust a black box.
