Snapshot of `keyhive-wasm` release tags taken via `gh api repos/inkandswitch/keyhive/releases`. First public alpha was `0.0.0-alpha.0` on 2025-09-22; cadence accelerated through Q4 2025 and Q1-Q2 2026, with `0.0.0-alpha.58b` shipped 2026-05-21. Every tag is flagged `prerelease: true` and the version number is stuck at `0.0.0`, signalling no semver-stable surface. Roughly 60 alpha cuts in eight months indicates active iteration on the Wasm/TS surface; combined with the README's "expect bugs, inconsistencies, and unstable APIs" disclaimer this is consistent with Ink & Switch's "show your work" research-engineering style rather than a stabilisation push toward v0.1.
