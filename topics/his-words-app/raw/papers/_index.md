Peer-reviewed academic sources from CHI / CSCW / PNAS and the foundational behavioral-psychology canon.

- 2026-06-23-psych-one-sec-self-nudge-pnas — Grüning, Riedel, Lorenz-Spreen *PNAS* 2023. Closest published analog to His Words: ~57% abandonment-at-pause, ~50% time reduction sustained past 6 weeks.
