## Mechanism

**Pray-to-unlock with mood input.** Per official description:

> "before instagram, tiktok, or games open, you pause, pray, and then unlock"

Flow:
1. User selects which apps to block.
2. App prompts: "tell god how you feel today" (mood selector).
3. App generates a short, Bible-rooted prayer matched to mood.
4. User prays the generated prayer.
5. Selected apps unlock.

**Repeatable** — "pray and lock your apps as many times as you want." This is interrupt-style, not one-time daily.

## Pricing

- **Subscription required for ALL features.** Free tier is functionally non-existent.
- App description states twice: *"all of the above features require an active subscription"* and *"prayer lock requires an active subscription; no features are available without one."*
- Specific tier amounts not disclosed in iTunes metadata; common pattern in this category is ~$5-10/mo or ~$50/yr.
- Free download with paywall on first launch.

## Platform

iOS only. Listed compatible with iPhone 5s through iPhone 17 Pro Max + iPad lineage. Languages: EN, ES.

## Notes

- **Subscription-only is aggressive.** No free tier means many curious users churn at paywall — opportunity for His Words to offer permanent free tier.
- **AI-generated prayers are theologically risky** — Reformed / confessional users distrust LLM "prayer generators" because they're not from canonical text. His Words could differentiate by being **scripture-only, no AI**.
- **Mood-matching is shallow personalization** — picks a prayer template from a small bank, not deep contextual understanding.
- Gamification (streaks, sheep growth) crosses into territory that some Christians find theologically uncomfortable ("works-based" framing of prayer habits).
- Active development + 30k ratings is a *real* moat. His Words must differentiate on mechanism (recurring N-min interrupt) and integrity (scripture-only, no AI), not feature breadth.
