## Mechanism (per description excerpts)

All variants converge on the same recipe with minor sauce changes:

- **FaithLocked (Gonzalez Martin)**: Blocks apps until you pray. Free daily verses + denominational devotionals. Premium adds AI-personalized prayers, guided devotionals, **family prayer features**.
- **FaithLock (Hangzhou Escape Universe)** — Chinese developer despite Christian branding: prompts prayer before social media, prayer streak tracking, "spiritual analytics."
- **Faith Lock: Bible Prayer Focus (Appbiz)**: "Stop Scrolling. Start Scripture." Daily verses, prayer tracking, scheduled lock times.
- **Faith Lock: Pray & Focus (Markovic)**: Marketed as "digital covenant," uses iOS Screen Time API for strict blocking; 90-day journey tracking.
- **Prayer Focus - FaithLock (APP EFFECTS UK)**: "Lock Distractions. Unlock Your Faith." Streaks, reflection space, scripture references.
- **Faith Lock (Evolve AI)**: AI Christian reflection companion with home-screen widgets (premium).
- **Faith Lock: Bible App Blocker (Prajapati)**: Solve **Bible verse puzzles** to unlock — interesting twist; "Beat the scroll one Scripture at a time."

## Notes

- **Name-collision is a moat-killer.** If His Words competes on naming or SEO, "FaithLock" / "Faith Lock" / "Prayer Lock" are exhausted. Brand defensibility requires a distinctive name (His Words is good; FaithLock is dead).
- **Hangzhou Escape Universe Technology Co. shipping Christian apps is a tell** — non-Christian operators farming the category with translated boilerplate. Likely scripture content quality is poor.
- **Six near-identical apps shipped within ~6 months (Jan-Jun 2026)** — this is a *gold rush*. The category was discovered in late 2025 (Prayer Lock's April 2025 launch was the catalyst), and now everyone is cloning. Window for differentiation is ~6 more months before commoditization deepens.
- **None offer recurring N-minute interrupts.** Every app reviewed uses *daily reset* or *per-session prayer* gates. His Words' recurring-interrupt mechanism is genuinely unoccupied.
- **None offer accountability / partner pairing in a serious way** — FaithLocked's "family prayer" is the only mention. Covenant Eyes (Victory Shield) owns the *secular* accountability category but for porn-blocking, not scripture-replacement.
