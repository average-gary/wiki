# Pray.com — the venture-backed meditation/prayer hybrid

## Founders & founding
- Founded **2016** by Steve Gatena, Michael Lynn, Ryan Beck, Matthew Potter.
- Steve Gatena (CEO) has publicly noted **VC bias against faith-based tech** as a fundraising obstacle.

## Funding rounds (disclosed)
| Date | Round | Amount | Lead Investors |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2017 | Seed | **$2M** | Science Inc., Greylock Partners, Spark Capital |
| March 2018 | Series A | **$14M** | TPG Growth (lead), Science Inc., Greylock |
| **Total disclosed** | | **~$16M** | |

**Note**: The original brief mentioned "Series B (~$75M raised) DCG-backed" — Wikipedia does not corroborate this. Either (a) Pray.com raised an undisclosed Series B not on Wikipedia, (b) the $75M number is total cumulative including unannounced rounds, or (c) the brief conflated it with another company. **Confidence: low on Series B claim**. Worth checking SEC Form D filings.

## Product & content
- Audio-first: prayers, meditations, sermons, podcasts, livestreamed services, bedtime Bible stories.
- High-profile narrators: **Kristen Bell, Blair Underwood**.
- 2024 partnerships: IndyCar sponsorship (driver Sting Ray Robb); AI collaboration with **Palantir Technologies** (notable — Peter Thiel adjacency).

## Growth signal
- COVID-19 produced "**955% download surge**" (specific baseline not given).
- Removed from Apple App Store in **China** for religious-content restrictions.

## Current state assessment
- Brief asks "declining? stable? acquired?" — public signals are mixed:
  - Still actively releasing partnerships (2024 IndyCar, Palantir AI).
  - No publicized Series B/C since 2018, suggesting either profitability, stealth raises, or struggles.
  - Confidence on current trajectory: **low** — needs deeper Crunchbase + LinkedIn employee-count check.

## Monetization
- Subscription model (premium meditations, bedtime stories) — pricing not stated in this source.
- Content licensing (Kristen Bell narration is a paid talent deal).

## Strategic takeaway for "His Words"
- Pray.com's audio-first + celebrity-narrator playbook is well-trodden.
- Faith-VC bias confirmed by Pray.com's CEO publicly — fundraising for "His Words" should expect 2-3x more outreach than secular wellness peers.
- AI-collab with Palantir suggests an emerging "AI prayer companion" frontier; could be threat or opportunity.
