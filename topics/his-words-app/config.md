# his-words-app config

## Scope

**In scope:**
- "His Words" — a faith-based digital wellness app concept whose differentiator is *time-based interruption* of social-media use (every N minutes) with a 60-second Scripture pause, rather than one-time gating.
- Direct competitors: Psalmo, Amen (faith), Holy Focus, BibleFocus, FaithLock, Sanctum, plus general digital-wellness app cousins (One Sec, Opal, Jomo, ScreenZen).
- Christian-app context: Hallow, YouVersion, Pray.com, Glorify, Bible Gateway — for market sizing, monetization, and "what big-tent Christian app users already have."
- Mobile platform feasibility: iOS Screen Time / Family Controls / DeviceActivity / ManagedSettings / Shield UI; Android Accessibility Service, UsageStatsManager, SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW, foreground services.
- Behavioral-psychology evidence: implementation intentions, habit substitution, pause prompts, attention-restoration, intermittent-rhythm vs. hard-block experiments.
- Bible content licensing & APIs: API.Bible, YouVersion API, Bible.org NET, ESV API, public-domain translations.
- Family / accountability prior art: Covenant Eyes, Bark, Accountable2You, Truple, Ever Accountable.

**Out of scope:**
- Building the actual codebase — that belongs in a `~/repos/his-words/.wiki/` if/when implementation starts.
- Theological commentary, sermon material, or doctrinal stances on social media.
- General Bible-software engineering (handled in `open-source-logos-suite`).
- Specific named children, congregations, or accountability-partner identities.

## Sensitivity

`public-oss`. The hub is publishable; this topic is a research compilation suitable for open sharing. If the project takes on commercial intent or proprietary specifications, fork into a repo-local `.wiki/` per the hub conventions.
