# Biblical miracle ↔ PF2e Remaster spell map

A lookup table for [[magic-theology-approaches]] approach #6 (hard-canon translation tables) — and useful as a flavor reference for any approach. ~50 mapped miracles, seeded from synthesis (no public fan table of this scope was findable; the *Testament* (Green Ronin 2003) and *Adventurer's Guide to the Bible* (Red Panda 2024) commercial PDFs ship private lists not openly indexed).

## How to use this table

- **Tradition** column: PF2e Remaster traditions. Where a miracle's cleanest spell fit is Arcane/Primal but the miracle is Divine-sourced, the **Notes** column flags the friction and the resolution path.
- **Sanctification** column: Holy / Unholy / either / N/A — useful for Sanctified casters (Champion / Cleric per [[class-ancestry-reskin-verdicts]]).
- **Custom-spell candidates** are flagged where no Remaster spell fits cleanly; see § "Design backlog" below.

## Tradition friction summary

The PF2e tradition system doesn't perfectly align with theological source. Three friction classes:

| Friction class | Examples | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| **Spell is Arcane/Primal but miracle is Divine** | Fireball (Elijah), Earthquake (Sinai/Jericho), Wall of Water (Red Sea), Tsunami (Flood), Blight (fig tree), Storm of Vengeance (Sinai) | (a) Grant via deity-domain spell list per [[yhwh-deity-template]] — Sarenrae has *Fireball* via Sun domain, set the same precedent for YHWH; (b) under approach #1 (Charism), fold into Divine via reskin; (c) under approach #2 (Lewisian), all four traditions are valid conduits |
| **Spell is Unholy-sanctified but miracle is Holy-source** | Darkness (plague), Slay Living (Passover, Ananias), Blight (fig tree), Animate Dead (Ezekiel bones) | The Bible's God *does* judge — these are theologically Holy-source but *destruction-targeted*. PF2e's Holy/Unholy axis isn't quite "good/evil"; it's "Empyreal/Abyssal coding." Solution: a Holy YHWH-cleric can cast an Unholy-coded spell when **the target is theologically liable to wrath** (a sanctification-bypass clause, similar to anathema waivers for Champions) |
| **No PF2e spell exists for the miracle** | Resurrection of Christ, Transfiguration (full glory), water-to-wine, Pillar of Cloud-and-Fire, Walls of Jericho | Either custom homebrew Divine spell (see "Design backlog" below), or treat as GM-fiat narrative event (Aquinas's degrees-of-miracle distinction in [[2026-05-25-aquinas-summa-q95-q96-divination-magic]] — first-degree miracles surpass nature absolutely and should not be castable) |

## Design backlog: custom-spell candidates

Flagged where no Remaster spell fits cleanly. Candidates for a future homebrew supplement:

1. **Plague of Blood** (rank 5 Divine) — water-to-blood area effect.
2. **Trumpet of Jericho** (rank 7 Divine ritual) — sonic damage to structures, 7-day buildup.
3. **Smite the Rock** (rank 4 Divine) — water from stone.
4. **Stand Still, Sun** (rank 10 Divine) — localized time-stop with cosmological flavor.
5. **Shekinah Pillar** (Divine ritual) — continuous *Obscuring Mist* by day, *Continual Flame* + *Light* by night.
6. **Peace, Be Still** (rank 5 Divine, instant) — instant *Control Weather*.
7. **Apostolic Shadow** (Divine focus power) — aura-of-healing tied to Champion-style faith mechanic.
8. **Hand of YHWH** (cantrip) — glowing Divine hand writes / gestures.
9. **Glorious Body Translation** (rank 9 Divine) — Plane Shift with bodily-translation flavor (Elijah, Christ's ascension).
10. **Angel of Death's Passover** (rank 8 ritual) — area *Slay Living* with sanctification-bypass for theologically-liable targets.
11. **Transmute Liquid** (rank 3 Divine) — water-to-wine (or analogous).
