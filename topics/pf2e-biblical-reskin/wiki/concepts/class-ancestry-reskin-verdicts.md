# Class & ancestry reskin verdicts

Per-class and per-ancestry verdicts for a PF2e Remaster Biblical reskin. Each verdict is one of: **Ship** (works as-is or with a major win), **Reflavor** (text changes only), **Rules-tweak** (small homebrew rule), **Cut**. Tied to a specific Remaster mechanic where possible.

## The four big wins

Confirmed; arguably understated in the original brief:

1. **Champion** — Sanctification (holy/unholy/spirit/void) + edicts/anathema is **literally a Beatitudes-tenets engine**. The Remaster's decoupling of cause from alignment did most of the design work.
2. **Thaumaturge** — Nine implements **map 1:1 to Christian relic categories** (chalice, regalia, tome, lantern, weapon, mirror, amulet, bell, wand-as-staff-of-Aaron). Zero rules change.
3. **Oracle** — Cursebound mechanic IS Jeremiah 20:9 ("a fire shut up in my bones"); mystery → calling. **Stronger OT-prophet fit than Cleric** in some respects.
4. **Nephilim ancestry** — Genesis 6 angelic-hybrid reading is canonical to second-temple Judaism and gameable. The Remaster's merger of Aasimar/Tiefling/Aphorite/Ganzi into a single Nephilim heritage makes it uniquely fit; see [[biblical-cosmology-pf2e-mapping]] § Nephilim.

## Approach-dependent classes

The verdicts above assume the **hybrid Charism + Lewisian default** ([[magic-theology-approaches]]). They shift under other approaches:

- **Strict-historicist** (Testament-style, approach #4): cut all of Wizard / Witch / Sorcerer / Magus / Summoner / Psychic / Druid / Bard. Keep only Cleric, Champion, Oracle, plus all martials. Dramatically simpler; fewer classes playable.
- **All-magic-demonic** (Reformed Augustinian, approach #3): same cuts as #4 but with the option of playing the cut classes as *fallen* characters with tragic-arc framing.
- **Pure charism** (#1, no Lewisian softening): cut Arcane and Occult cleanly. Wizard and Psychic gone; Witch gone. Sorcerer keeps Angelic bloodline only.

## Champion: the structural win

The Champion class deserves its own subsection. Per [[2026-05-24-class-ancestry-reskin-aon-champion-sanctification]] and [[2026-05-24-remaster-fit-for-monotheism-aon-champion-class]]:

- Sanctification is now **holy / unholy / none** — not Lawful Good / Chaotic Good etc.
- **Cause is decoupled from alignment.** Justice (paladin), Mercy (redeemer), Liberation (liberator) are the cause-style tenets but they're now selectable independently of any alignment grid.
- Edicts and anathema attach to the *deity*, not the cause.
- Atone ritual is a generic mechanic, not alignment-locked.

This means a Christian Champion is built by:
1. Pick deity = YHWH (or specific saint patron in Catholic/Orthodox lens).
2. Pick sanctification = holy.
3. Pick cause = Justice / Mercy / Liberation (= Beatitude-tenets).
4. Edicts/anathema = Decalogue + Sermon on the Mount + denomination-specific items.

**Total custom-rule footprint: zero.** Everything is reflavor.

## Oracle: the surprise win

The Oracle's **cursebound** mechanic — power that comes with progressively worsening physical/social cost — is structurally identical to the OT prophet experience. Jeremiah 20:9 ("if I say I will not mention his name... there is in my heart as it were a burning fire shut up in my bones, and I am weary with holding it in"). Hosea's marriage. Ezekiel's silence and sign-acts.

The Remaster's **mystery → calling** reframe (the "mystery" doesn't have to be polytheistic — it's just "the source from which your gift came") makes Oracle directly playable as Old Testament prophet. **No rules change needed.**

## Thaumaturge: the elegant win

The Thaumaturge's nine implements (chalice, regalia, tome, lantern, weapon, mirror, amulet, bell, wand) map 1:1 to Christian relic categories that already exist in tradition:

- **Chalice** → Eucharistic chalice / Holy Grail tradition
- **Regalia** → episcopal vestments / Aaronic priestly garments
- **Tome** → the Bible itself, or a saint's *vita*
- **Lantern** → "Thy word is a lamp unto my feet" (Ps 119:105); paschal candle
- **Weapon** → St Michael's sword / Holy Lance / staff of Aaron
- **Mirror** → "we see through a glass darkly" (1 Cor 13:12)
- **Amulet** → reliquary pendant / scapular / phylactery
- **Bell** → liturgical bell / sanctus bell
- **Wand** → staff of Moses / shepherd's crook

**Pilgrim/saint-who-wields-sacred-objects is a clean character class.** No rules change.

## Sorcerer: the bloodline-cut win

Per [[2026-05-24-class-ancestry-reskin-aon-magic-classes-arcane]], the Sorcerer's **Angelic bloodline** explicitly grants divine tradition with descent-from-celestials flavor. This is the canonical sorcerer reskin: the sorcerer is descended from a faithful (or formerly-faithful) Watcher, and their power is hereditary access to divine miracles.

**Bloodlines to keep**: Angelic, Aberrant (reframed as Watcher-corrupted, villain-leaning), Imperial (royal Davidic line), Wyrmblood (Leviathan/Behemoth descent — Job 40-41), Genie (faithful elemental Watchers), Psychopomp (death-attendant angels — gameable).

**Bloodlines to cut**: Demonic, Diabolic, Hag, Shadow, Undead. (The Demonic and Diabolic could survive as villain-only NPCs.)
