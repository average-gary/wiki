# Biblical cosmology mapped to PF2e planes and creatures

A reference for which Biblical concepts go where in the PF2e Remaster planar/creature taxonomy. Picks denominational specifics deliberately — see [[denominational-lens-decision]] for which lens to commit to.

## Cosmology — the layered cosmos

Two end-states. Present age is layered; eschaton is unified.

**Present age (three tiers)**:
- **Heavens** — the throne room of Rev 4–5; the Third Heaven of 2 Cor 12. The "firmament" is the older OT cosmographic shorthand.
- **Earth** — the Material Plane.
- **Sheol / Hades / Gehenna / Tartarus / Abyss** — the lower realms (collapsed in popular usage but distinct in scripture). See below.

**Eschaton (unified)**:
- The **New Jerusalem** descends (Rev 21–22).
- New Heavens and New Earth — restoration, not abandonment, of creation. Catholic, Orthodox, Anglican, and N.T. Wright-school Protestants emphasize this; some dispensationalists prefer annihilation-and-recreation. See [[denominational-lens-decision]].

## Plane mapping (PF2e Remaster)

| Biblical concept | PF2e plane | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Heaven** (Throne Room, Rev 4–5; Third Heaven, 2 Cor 12) | upper celestial planes (Heaven / Nirvana renamed) | The Empyrean. Not a generic "good plane"; the actual seat of the throne. |
| **Sheol** (OT morally-neutral abode of the dead) | Boneyard ferry zone | Pre-judgment intermediate state. |
| **Hades** (Greek-coded LXX rendering of Sheol) | same as Sheol | Same realm, NT-era vocabulary. |
| **Gehenna** (Valley of Hinnom; eschatological punishment) | Hell (lawful evil layer renamed) | Final-state punishment for the unrepentant. |
| **Tartarus** (2 Pet 2:4) | Tartarus floor of the Abyss | Specifically a prison for fallen angels. |
| **The Abyss / `tehom`** (Gen 1:2; Rev 9, 20) | the Abyss / sealed-titan zones (Pit of Gormuz analog) | Cosmic prison for chaos / Watchers. Distinct from final hell. |
| **Earth** | Material Plane | |
| **New Jerusalem / New Heavens-and-Earth** | post-eschaton state of the Material Plane fused with Heaven | **Campaign endgame state** — high-level capstone. |
| **Purgatory** (Catholic) / **Aerial Toll Houses** (Orthodox) | transitional plane / multi-level testing zone | Denomination-specific; see [[denominational-lens-decision]]. Toll Houses make for a literal level-15+ post-mortem mega-dungeon. |

## Angelic hierarchy → PF2e celestial chain

The **nine-rank Pseudo-Dionysian hierarchy** (~5th–6th century, *De Coelesti Hierarchia*; systematized by Aquinas in *Summa* I.108) is **traditional, not dogmatic**. Catholic teaching uses it but binds nothing. Three triads:

| Triad | Choirs | PF2e mapping |
|---|---|---|
| **Contemplative** (closest to God) | Seraphim, Cherubim, Thrones | Solar / Empyrean tier; biblical-form Cherubim (four-faced, Ezek 1) NOT putti |
| **Governance** (cosmic order) | Dominions, Virtues, Powers | Planetar / astral deva tier with governance/miracle/war specialization |
| **Engaged** (earthly affairs) | Principalities, Archangels, Angels | Principalities = nation-guardians (Dan 10); Archangels = unique named (Michael/Gabriel/Raphael); Angels = guardian angels (cassisian-tier) |

**Named archangels — denominational split**:
- **Catholic**: 3 (Michael, Gabriel, Raphael — Raphael from Tobit, deuterocanonical)
- **Eastern Orthodox**: 7 (+ Uriel, Selaphiel, Jegudiel, Barachiel)
- **Protestant**: usually 2 (Michael, Gabriel); Raphael contested due to Tobit's deuterocanonical status.
- **Uriel** is the swing case — appears in 2 Esdras (deuterocanonical) and 1 Enoch (non-canonical to most Christians).

## Demonic origins — two parallel traditions

Both should be aware in any reskin. They were originally distinct and are sometimes synthesized:

1. **Lucifer's fall** — patristic reading of Isa 14 / Ezek 28; pride as the original sin; Michael casting the dragon down (Rev 12). Catholic and Orthodox mainstream; Reformed mainstream.
2. **Watchers tradition** — 1 Enoch's fallen angels descending on Mt. Hermon, taking human wives, teaching forbidden arts (metallurgy, weapons, cosmetics, sorcery — the *pharmakeia* genealogy), fathering Nephilim. Cited obliquely in Jude 6 / 2 Pet 2:4. **Non-canonical to most Christians but enormously influential on the imagery.**

### PF2e fiend-type mapping

The PF2e three-track fiend system maps onto the Christian double-origin remarkably well:

- **Devils** → Lucifer-fall hierarchy. Lawful, court-of-Hell, named (Beelzebub, Asmodeus, Mammon, Belial). Pride-fall; rule-bound deception; legalistic torment.
- **Demons** → Watcher-fall hierarchy. Chaotic, primordial, Nephilim-spawning, named (Shemyaza, Azazel). Lust-fall; flesh-corruption; instinctive violence.
- **Daemons** → plague/famine/death personifications drawn from Revelation horsemen. Death-aligned; impersonal; eschatological scourge.

This is a stronger mapping than the OGL/Golarion default, which arbitrarily split fiends along an alignment grid that no longer exists.

### Esoteric layer to flag, not import

*Lesser Key of Solomon* (Goetia, 72 demons), Wierus's *Pseudomonarchia Daemonum*, medieval grimoires give elaborate demon-name catalogues (Asmodeus, Belial, Mammon, Astaroth, Bael, Paimon, etc.). **These are not Biblical** and are classified by orthodox traditions as superstition or worse. They are, however, what PF2e's demon roster (and Goetia-derived popular fiction) actually draws from. **Recommendation**: use Biblical/deuterocanonical names where they exist (Beelzebub, Asmodeus from Tobit, Belial from 2 Cor 6:15); flag grimoire-only names as in-world apocrypha that PCs encounter as forbidden books, not as Author Truth.

## Nephilim — three readings, pick one

Genesis 6:1–4 + Numbers 13:33. Three competing readings:

1. **Sethite/Cainite intermarriage** — Augustine's reading; "sons of God" = Sethite line, "daughters of men" = Cainite line. Reformed-friendly. Less gameable.
2. **Angelic-human hybrids** — 1 Enoch's reading; second-temple Jewish consensus. **Most worldbuilders pick this** because it's gameable.
3. **Royal "men of renown" hyperbole** — modern critical-historical reading. Not gameable.

**Recommendation for the PF2e reskin**: run reading #2 (angelic-hybrid). Maps directly onto the **Nephilim ancestry** (the Remaster merger of Aasimar / Tiefling / Aphorite / Ganzi). Half-Watcher heritage, giant-blooded, ancestral guilt + ancestral power. This is the **single biggest ancestry-reskin win in the Remaster** — see [[class-ancestry-reskin-verdicts]].

## Deuterocanon contributions to lore

Even Protestant-frame settings should know what's in the deuterocanon, because the imagery comes from there:

- **Tobit** — Raphael as named archangel (the only NT/deutero-canonical text to name him); Asmodeus as named demon.
- **2 Maccabees** — martyrs, prayers for the dead (feeds purgatory doctrine).
- **Wisdom of Solomon** — Lady Wisdom personified (gameable as a cosmic manifestation, possibly a quasi-deity / herald or empyreal lord).
- **1 Enoch** — Watchers, Nephilim. Cited in Jude 14–15. Non-canonical to most Christians but the source of the imagery everyone uses.

## Eschatology — four campaign-shape variables

A worldbuilder must pick on each axis. They constrain campaign mechanics and endgame:

| Axis | Options | Gameplay implication |
|---|---|---|
| **Millennium reading** | Pre-, Post-, A-millennial | Pre = literal apocalypse-then-kingdom adventure path; A = present age of spiritual conflict; Post = optimistic gradual world-transformation |
| **Intermediate state** | Catholic purgatory / Orthodox prayers-for-dead / Protestant immediate beatific vision | Purgatory = transitional plane; immediate-vision = no intermediate playspace |
| **Cosmos at the end** | Restoration vs annihilation-and-recreation | Restoration (dominant Catholic/Orthodox/Wright) = high-level endgame is creation healed; annihilation = creation replaced (dispensationalist) |
| **Judgment** | Particular (at death) vs general (eschatological) | Both happen in most traditions; question is which is gameable foreground |

**Recommended default for a wide-audience reskin**: amillennial + restoration + both judgments + Catholic-or-Orthodox intermediate state. This gives the richest campaign material (Aerial Toll Houses, purgatory, restored Material Plane endgame) without committing to dispensational specifics.
