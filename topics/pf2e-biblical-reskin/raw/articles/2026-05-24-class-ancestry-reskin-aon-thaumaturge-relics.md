# Thaumaturge (PF2e)

## Why this is the best relic-class fit in PF2e
The Thaumaturge "scavenged the best parts of every magical tradition" to exploit supernatural weaknesses with mystical objects. Cha-key class. Nine implements available, three obtained over career (levels 1, 5, 15), each with Initiate → Adept → Paragon scaling.

## Mechanical features that survive untouched
- **Exploit Vulnerability** (1 action, 1/round): roll Esoteric Lore vs DC. On success, apply mortal weakness or Personal Antithesis (custom weakness 2+ ½ level). Reflavor: "discerning the spirits" (1 John 4:1) — relic reveals the true nature of the foe (demon, idolater, false prophet).
- **Implement's Empowerment**: +2 damage per weapon die. Reflavor: anointing.
- **Esoterica**: smaller mystic objects brandished as bonus material. Reflavor: prayer beads, splinters of relic-sticks, vials of myrrh, blessed salt, sand from Holy Land.

## Reskin verdict
**Pure reflavor; zero rules changes.** Pilgrim/saint-aspirant/relic-bearer. Recommended fluff: the character has assembled an itinerant relic-bag and intercedes through them. Pairs naturally with the Champion (knight-pilgrim) and the Cleric (relic-empowered priest).

The brief's instinct to elevate Thaumaturge as a top-tier reskin win is correct and arguably underrated — this class IS the medieval reliquary-cult mechanically codified.
