## Profile

- Publisher: **Red Panda Publishing** ("and Bible enthusiasts").
- Format: **350-page 5E-compatible setting + adventure module**.
- Setting: **1st-century AD Levant during Jesus's earthly ministry**.
- Content: 4 new lineages, multiple subclasses, adventures levels 1–10, NPCs, monsters, locations.

## Design approach

- Translates biblical figures into **D&D-style stat blocks and abilities** that reflect their scriptural roles, rather than direct catechesis.
- Functions as a **sandbox** allowing players to explore biblical narratives via familiar tabletop RPG mechanics.
- Reviewer comment: **"The material was sufficiently true to the source material while also being a heck of a lot of fun."**

## What this confirms

The "Testament solve" scales forward into the 5E era and is a viable contemporary niche: keep the host system's rules, supply biblical setting, characters, and locations, let DMs handle theological tone at the table.

## Relevance

A direct modern proof-of-concept for the PF2e biblical reskin: a Bible-themed setting layered onto a major existing system can find an audience and reviewers describe it as both faithful and fun — the dual evaluation Christian RPGs almost always have to pass.
