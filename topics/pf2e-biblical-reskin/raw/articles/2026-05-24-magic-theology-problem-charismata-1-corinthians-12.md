# The Nine Charismata

| Gift | Function | PF2e Mapping (provisional) |
|---|---|---|
| Word of wisdom | Spirit-guided counsel | Oracle Mystery: Lore; Bardic inspiration |
| Word of knowledge | Spirit-given facts | *Detect Magic*, *Detect Lies*, *Read Aura*, *Augury* |
| Faith | Mountain-moving trust | Champion's *Lay on Hands*, devotion auras |
| Healings | Restoration of body | *Heal*, *Cure Wounds*, *Restoration*, *Remove Disease* |
| Miracles | Direct divine intervention | *Miracle*, *Holy Light*, *Banishment* |
| Prophecy | Foretelling / forth-telling | *Augury*, *Foresight*, *Vision* |
| Discernment of spirits | Perception of hidden agencies | *See Invisibility*, *Detect Alignment*, *Glimpse Soul* |
| Tongues | Spirit-given languages | *Comprehend Languages*, *Tongues* |
| Interpretation | Render the unknown known | *Translate*, *Comprehend* |

## Theological Distinctions from Magic

1. **Source**: Holy Spirit, not occult force or personal training.
2. **Earnability**: "Cannot be earned or merited" — opposed to Vancian-prep / wizard-study.
3. **Bearer**: Manifestation *of the Spirit*, not of the gifted person — the gift is borrowed, not owned.
4. **Purpose**: Communal edification, not personal benefit.
5. **Mode**: Petition and submission, not technique and command.

## Implication for a Christian Reskin
Provides an explicit list to map PF2e Divine spells onto. Clean fit for: Cleric, Champion, Oracle (with reskinned Mysteries), some Bard (prophet/psalmist). Hard fit for: Wizard (study), Witch (patron), Sorcerer (bloodline = blood pact?), Magus (sword + spell = ritual technician), Summoner (binds eidolon = familiar spirit).
