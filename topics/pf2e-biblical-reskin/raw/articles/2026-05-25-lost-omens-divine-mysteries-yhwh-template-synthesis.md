# YHWH deity entry skeleton

## Hard structural constraints (from Remaster)

| Field | Length / shape | Notes |
|-------|----------------|-------|
| Name + epithet | "YHWH (The Lord of Hosts)" or similar | Epithet is mandatory in Remaster style |
| Narrative blurb | 140-200 words | Pharasma was longest at ~190; Erastil shortest at ~140 |
| Edicts | **3-4 short imperative phrases** | Comma-joined, no list bullets in stat block |
| Anathema | **3-4 short prohibitions** | Mix metaphysical taboos and character violations |
| Area of Concern | 3-5 short nouns | "and" before final item: "X, Y, Z, and W" |
| Divine Attribute | 2 attributes (e.g., Wis or Cha) | Used by Raised by Belief background |
| Divine Font | Heal / Harm / Heal or Harm | Tracks sanctification |
| Sanctification | one of: holy / unholy / both / none | **Pharasma precedent**: can be "none" |
| Divine Skill | exactly one | |
| Favored Weapon | exactly one | |
| Domains | **4 primary + 1-3 alternate** | Drawn from canonical PF2 domain list |
| Cleric Spells | **exactly 3, level-tagged** | Typical levels: 1st, 3rd, 5th |
| Religious symbol | 1 short phrase | Remaster-only field |
| Sacred animal | 1 noun | Remaster-only field |
| Sacred colors | 1-2 colors | Remaster-only field |

## Recommended fills (denominational-lens-aware)

### Common across all denominational lenses

- **Name**: "YHWH" or "The Lord" (use Tetragrammaton conservatively; Adonai works as epithet)
- **Epithet**: "I AM" / "The Almighty" / "Lord of Hosts" — pick one, save the others for cleric spells or boons
- **Divine Attribute**: Wisdom or Charisma (matches the prophetic + kingly biblical archetype)
- **Divine Font**: **Heal** (universally; Harm is theologically wrong for YHWH except possibly in a divine-warrior reading — see Reformed variant)
- **Divine Skill**: Religion (obvious) — though Diplomacy or Society are defensible
- **Favored Weapon**: This is the hardest field. Candidates: **sling** (David, "the Lord is my shepherd"), **shepherd's crook** (could be reskinned as a club/staff), **two-handed sword** (Hebrews 4:12 "sharper than any two-edged sword" — best mechanical fit, longsword stats), **trumpet/shofar** as exotic. Recommend **longsword** with flavor "sword of the Word" and shofar as flavor for the major boon.
- **Domains** (pick 4 primary): from PF2 canonical list, the closest fits are **Creation, Family, Healing, Knowledge, Sun, Truth, Tyranny** (in the dominion sense), **Zeal, Confidence, Vigil, Soul, Indulgence** (covenant abundance), **Death, Fate, Repose**. Recommend primary: **Creation, Truth, Healing, Zeal**. Alternate: **Family, Knowledge, Soul, Vigil**.
- **Religious symbol**: cross (Christian-anchored), or burning bush / ark / shofar (Hebrew Bible-anchored) — denomination-dependent
- **Sacred animal**: lamb (universally Christian) or dove (Spirit) or eagle (Isaiah 40:31)
- **Sacred colors**: gold, white, red (passion/blood) — pick 2

### Denominational-lens variants (cross-ref [[denominational-lens-decision]])

**Catholic lens**:
- Sanctification: **can choose holy** (champion path = paladin/crusader is core Catholic imagination — Templars, Joan of Arc, Pope's military orders historically)
- Edicts: "love God with all your heart, love your neighbor as yourself, honor the sacraments, defend the Church and the poor"
- Anathema: "deny the Real Presence, take innocent life, abandon the sacraments, despair of God's mercy"
- Domains add: **Family** (importance of the domestic church)

**Reformed lens**:
- Sanctification: **can choose holy** but discourage; emphasize the cleric is *minister of Word and sacrament*, not crusader
- Edicts: "preach the Word in season and out, love God and neighbor, do justice, walk humbly"
- Anathema: "worship idols (images, doctrines, self), despair of grace, oppress the poor, presume upon God's mercy"
- **Notably more Word-centric** — favored weapon could lean longsword (Word-as-sword)
- Could also justify Harm-or-Heal duality (theologia crucis: God's wrath and mercy are one act in Christ); but **Heal** is still simpler

**Pentecostal/Charismatic lens**:
- Sanctification: **can choose holy** (Spirit baptism = real mechanical empowerment)
- Edicts: "obey the Spirit's leading, lay hands on the sick, proclaim freedom to captives, pursue holiness"
- Anathema: "grieve the Spirit, deny the gifts, profane the gathered church, love money"
- **Cleric Spells** could lean toward healing/exorcism: 1st *Heal*, 3rd *Holy Light*, 5th *Banishment*
- Sacred animal: dove (Spirit) over lamb

**Eastern Orthodox lens** (cross-ref [[2026-05-24-denominational-scope-theosis]]):
- Sanctification: can choose holy
- Edicts emphasize theosis: "pursue union with God, keep the fasts, venerate the saints, give alms"
- Anathema: "blaspheme the icons, despair of theosis, schism from the Body, presume upon grace"

### Pharasma-precedent variant: sanctification = none

**Worth considering** for a *strict* monotheistic reskin where the holy/unholy mechanic feels theologically wrong (e.g., refusing to mechanize sanctity): set sanctification to **none**, accept that no champion path exists, and require Christian-themed champions to take a *different* deity-stand-in (e.g., a saint, an angel, or "the cause of righteousness" abstracted).
- This is the most theologically rigorous option but the most mechanically restrictive.
- Documented as a real Paizo precedent (Pharasma), so it doesn't break PF2 design contracts.

## Edicts/Anathema drafting heuristics

1. **3-4 items each**, never more, rarely fewer.
2. Comma-joined imperative phrases, not full sentences.
3. Mix at least one **positive duty** with at least one **prohibition of intent/character** ("tell lies", "abandon a companion", "despair of mercy").
4. Avoid Iron-Age literal phrasing — abstract to the underlying ethical violation (Asmodeus precedent: "share power with the weak" not "free a slave").
5. At least one item should connect to the deity's specific *cosmological* role (Sarenrae's "destroy Spawn of Rovagug"; YHWH's might be "proclaim the gospel to all nations" or "uphold the covenant").
