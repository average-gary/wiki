# Sarenrae (deity-292) — Divine Mysteries pg. 94

- **Domains** (primary, 4): Fire, Healing, Sun, Truth
- **Alternate Domains** (1): Repose
- **Cleric Spells** (3, level-tagged): 1st Breathe Fire, 3rd Fireball, 4th Wall of Fire
- **Religious symbol**: angelic ankh; **sacred animal**: dove; **sacred colors**: blue, gold
- **Narrative blurb**: ~150 words, character-of-worship (boundless love, exquisite kindness, true patience; "oppose evil with words first, scimitar second")

## Pattern observations

- **Edicts**: 3-4 short imperative phrases joined by commas. Mix of positive duties ("protect allies") with situational/cosmological imperatives ("destroy the Spawn of Rovagug").
- **Anathema**: 3-4 short prohibitions; mix of metaphysical taboos ("create undead") and character violations ("lie", "dishonor yourself"). Tellingly, **Remaster anathema include lifestyle and character integrity prohibitions, not merely metaphysical evil acts.**
- **Sanctification phrasing**: "can choose holy" (optional alignment with the holy/unholy mechanic — opens champion paths but doesn't mandate them).
- **4 primary + 1 alternate** is the standard domain count for a major deity.
- **Cleric Spells** are exactly 3, with level signposts (1st / 3rd / 4th typically) — picked thematically.
- Remaster adds **religious_symbol**, **sacred_animal**, **sacred_color** fields not present in legacy CRB entries.
