# Kineticist + Summoner — element-channeler and angelic-companion

## Kineticist (ID=23, Con-key, NON-spellcasting)
- Internal "kinetic gate" channels elemental power.
- Single Gate (one element, deeper) vs Dual Gate (two elements, versatile).
- Elements: air, earth, fire, metal, water, wood.
- Kinetic Aura emanation; impulses (Elemental Blast, Base Kinesis); Gate Junctions; Channel Elements action.

**Why this class is unexpectedly biblical**: NOT a spellcaster — uses Constitution, no spell tradition. The character's body itself is the channel for elemental power. This is much closer to Hebrew miracle-narrative than to Wizard spellcasting. The miracle-workers of the OT (Moses, Elijah, Elisha) DON'T cast — they call upon God, who acts through the physical world.

**Reskin paths**:
1. **Prophet-of-elements**: Moses (water, parting sea, plagues), Elijah (fire on Carmel, drought, whirlwind chariot), Elisha (water purification, oil multiplication), Jonah (wind/storm). Single Gate (Fire) = Elijah build. Single Gate (Water) = Moses build.
2. **Edenic dominion-bearer**: pre-Fall mastery over creation, a remnant gift.
3. **Theological reframe**: the gate is NOT a power-source within the character but a covenantal channel — the prophet calls, God answers, the elements obey ("even the wind and the seas obey him" — Mark 4:41 echoing the OT pattern).

**Verdict**: KEEP with reframe (no rules changes; only narrative source-of-power retag). Wood and Metal elements are the strangest fit for biblical setting; consider restricting to Air/Earth/Fire/Water (the classical four). Restrict Dual Gate Wood+Metal at table discretion.

## Summoner (ID=18, Cha-key, eidolon-paired)
- Player and eidolon share HP, actions, senses.
- Eidolon types include angel, beast, construct, demon, devil, dragon, fey, phantom, plant, psychopomp, undead.
- Tandem Actions; Link Spells; spell tradition derives from eidolon type.

**Reskin verdict**: this is genuinely tricky. "Summoning" carries necromantic/conjuration baggage (Saul + Witch of Endor, 1 Sam 28). The class IS a summoner whether or not we like the word.

Three options:
1. **Guardian angel companion** (only the **Angel** eidolon survives; all others cut). Reframe as Tobit-style guardianship (Raphael accompanying Tobias). The eidolon is NOT summoned; it has accompanied the character since youth and is sometimes-visible. Mechanically: "Manifest" stays, but narratively becomes "becomes visible" rather than "is called from elsewhere."
2. **Cut entirely**.
3. **Severely restrict** to Angel and possibly Phantom (if phantoms are reframed as departed-saints visible to the character — fragile).

**Recommend option 1 with hard restriction to Angel-only eidolon.** The angel-eidolon is the cleanest divine-companion in PF2e and lets the setting deliver Tobit/Acts-12-Peter-released-by-angel imagery without inventing new mechanics.

The brief's instinct (guardian-angel companion) is correct.
