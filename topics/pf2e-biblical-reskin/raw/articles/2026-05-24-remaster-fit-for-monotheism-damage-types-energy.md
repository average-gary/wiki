# Remaster damage taxonomy — monotheism analysis

## The mapping (legacy → Remaster)

| Legacy | Remaster | Theological gloss |
|---|---|---|
| good damage | holy damage | aligned with God |
| evil damage | unholy damage | aligned with the demonic |
| lawful damage | (removed) | no theological axis in monotheism |
| chaotic damage | (removed) | no theological axis in monotheism |
| positive energy | vitality | life-force, divine creation sustaining |
| negative energy | void | anti-life, entropy, demonic |
| force vs incorporeal | spirit | soul-affecting (saving angels, harming demons) |

## Why this is a major win for monotheism

1. **Two-axis alignment damage is gone**: legacy PF2e had four damage types (good/evil/lawful/chaotic) baked into the cosmology. Lawful and Chaotic have **no Christian theological correlate** — God is not "lawful" in a way the devil is "chaotic." The Remaster removed both.
2. **Holy vs unholy is the right axis**: this maps directly onto God-vs-Satan, sanctified-vs-corrupted. It is a *binary*, which monotheism wants.
3. **Vitality vs void replaces positive vs negative**: theologically richer — vitality is "life from God"; void is "the privation of being" (good Augustinian metaphysics). No longer "two opposing planes of energy" but "creation vs entropy."
4. **Spirit damage** handles soul-affecting attacks generically — angels banishing demons, demons tormenting souls — without requiring an alignment label.

## What the GM still has to do

- Mark all surviving evil-sources as **unholy** (already done by Paizo for fiends).
- Mark divine attacks/blessings as **holy** where appropriate.
- Replace any homebrew lawful/chaotic damage with holy/unholy or untyped.
- Decide: are undead **void**-aligned, **unholy**-aligned, or both? (Default: void, unless they're cult undead serving demons, in which case both.)

## Open question

- The vitality-vs-void axis is metaphysical not moral: a vampire is unholy AND void; an angel is holy AND vitality-touched; but a tree is vitality without being holy. Keep these axes orthogonal in the reskin or you'll re-create the old "good = positive energy" conflation that PF2e legacy had.
