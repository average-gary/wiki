# Champion (PF2e Remaster)

## Core identity
Champions are "emissaries of a deity, devoted servants" combining combat with protective abilities for allies. Strength or Dexterity primary; 10 HP + Con per level.

## Sanctification system (the key reskin lever)
At 1st level the champion takes ONE sanctification, which determines the holy/unholy trait granted to the character and tightens the edict/anathema list:
- **Holy** — gain the holy trait. Edicts: protect innocents. Anathema: murder.
- **Unholy** — gain the unholy trait. Edicts: prioritize self/deity. Anathema: pure altruism.
- **Spirit/Void** — neutral-aligned options.

This replaces the old alignment-coded "Tenets of Good." The brief's hypothesis (Beatitudes become tenets) maps cleanly: "blessed are the merciful" → champion's-reaction-style protective interpose; "blessed are the peacemakers" → Redeemer cause; "blessed are those persecuted" → Liberator cause.

## Cause and devotion
Each champion picks a deity AND a cause; cause adds further edicts/anathema and unlocks the Champion's Reaction (improves into Relentless Reaction and Exalted Reaction at higher tiers). Devotion focus spells include `lay on hands`, `shields of the spirit`, `touch of the void` — `lay on hands` ports directly to a saint/healer-knight.

## Blessing of the Devoted (level 3)
Choose: Blessed Armament (weapon rune), Blessed Shield (reinforcing rune), Blessed Swiftness (move bonus). All three are mechanically agnostic and reflavor as anointed-weapon / consecrated-shield / pilgrim-pace with zero rule changes.

## Reskin verdict for biblical setting
- Crusader/Knight Hospitaller/Defender of the Faithful = pure reflavor, **no rule changes**.
- Anchorite/Ascetic-warrior = use Liberator or Redeemer cause + Blessed Swiftness.
- Anathema list literally ALREADY reads as a moral code; substituting Decalogue + denominational specifics is a 1:1 swap.
- The single biggest win: the brief's Beatitudes-as-tenets idea is explicitly supported by the rules architecture (edicts/anathema are designed to be deity-customized).
