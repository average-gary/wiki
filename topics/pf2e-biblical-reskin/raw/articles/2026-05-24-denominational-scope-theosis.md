# Theosis

## Three stages
1. **Katharsis** (purification — moral cleansing, ascetic struggle)
2. **Theoria** (illumination — direct experiential knowledge of God)
3. **Theosis** (union — participation in divine life)

## Essence/energies distinction
Gregory Palamas's key move: humans participate in God's uncreated *energies* (operations, grace, light) but never God's essence. Resolves the paradox of how creatures can become "partakers of the divine nature" (2 Pet 1:4) without becoming God.

## Uncreated light
Tabor light (Transfiguration) and the visions of hesychast monks — physical, perceptible, but uncreated. The hesychast saint at the peak of contemplation literally glows.

## Hesychasm and the Jesus Prayer
"Lord Jesus Christ, Son of God, have mercy on me, a sinner" — repeated continuously, joined to breath, descending from intellect into the heart. Mount Athos, 14th-century Palamite controversy.
