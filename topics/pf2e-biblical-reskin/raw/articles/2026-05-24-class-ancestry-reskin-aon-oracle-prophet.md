# Oracle (PF2e Remaster)

## Why the OT prophet fit is canonical, not strained
Oracles "access divine power through mysteries — potent concepts or ideals — rather than serving specific deities." This is precisely the prophet relationship: not a clergy-of contract but called against one's will to bear a divine concept (judgment, exile, comfort, restoration).

## Cursebound mechanic = prophet's burden
- Using cursebound abilities increases the cursebound condition (1–4 by level). Only Refocus removes it.
- This IS Jeremiah 20:9: "His word is in my heart like a fire shut up in my bones; I am weary of holding it in." The prophet pays a personal cost to deliver the word.
- Revelation Spells (focus, 1 FP each) become specific prophetic acts (Elisha making iron float; Elijah's fire; Hosea's broken-marriage parable; Ezekiel's bone-valley).

## Mystery → biblical calling map (suggested, no rules change)
- Battle → Joshua / Deborah (warrior-prophet)
- Bones → Ezekiel (valley of dry bones)
- Cosmos → Daniel (apocalyptic visions)
- Flames → Elijah (Carmel fire)
- Life → Elisha (Shunammite's son)
- Lore → Solomon-as-prophet / Daniel
- Tempest → Jonah / Moses-at-Red-Sea
- Ashes → Jeremiah (Lamentations)

## Reskin verdict
**Best-in-class fit for OT prophet without ANY mechanical changes.** Stronger than Cleric for the prophet archetype because: (1) Cha-not-Wis fits charismatic calling; (2) curse mechanic is theologically right (prophecy as suffering); (3) divine tradition without deity-edict bondage matches the OT pattern of prophets standing OUTSIDE the official priesthood (Amos: "I was no prophet, neither was I a prophet's son; but I was a herdsman").

## Caveat
The "mystery" should be reframed as *calling*, never as an impersonal force. The setting must specify these mysteries are facets of YHWH's self-revelation, not free-floating divine archetypes (Platonic forms drift toward heresy if unchecked).
