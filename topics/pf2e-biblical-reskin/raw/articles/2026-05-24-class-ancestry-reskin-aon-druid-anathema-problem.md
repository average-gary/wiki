# Druid (PF2e Remaster)

## Mechanics summary
- Wis-key, primal tradition, prepared casting.
- Druidic Order (animal/leaf/storm/untamed/wild — gain class feat, order spell, trained skill).
- Anathema (universal, all druids): despoil natural places; consume more natural resources than needed to live comfortably; teach Wildsong to non-druids.
- Each order adds further anathema.
- Animal Empathy or Plant Empathy at 1st.

## Why this class is the hardest reskin
Druid anathema reads like a vow — that part is GREAT for a Franciscan/Edenic-keeper reskin (replace "don't teach Wildsong" with "don't speak the unfallen tongue" or "don't divulge the rule of your order").

The PROBLEM is the magic source. "Primal tradition" is explicitly the magic of nature spirits / fey / elementals — a polytheist/animist framework. In a strict-monotheist setting, miraculous power over nature MUST originate from God, not from "nature itself" speaking back.

## Three viable reskin paths
1. **Edenic remnant / pre-Fall keeper**: druids are descendants of the unfallen line who retained pre-Curse stewardship over creation. Primal tradition = "Adamic dominion" partially preserved. Anathema retained as keeping the unfallen way. Cuts well with Leshy ancestry (see ancestry path).
2. **Franciscan friar / desert hermit / John the Baptist**: nature-mystic whose miracles come BY divine grace mediated through wilderness ascesis. Re-source the primal tradition as "divine tradition through wilderness." Mechanically: keep spell list, retag tradition.
3. **Cut entirely**: if the setting forbids mediated nature-magic, the Druid is sacrificed. Players who want the niche route through Cleric-Nature-Domain or Ranger.

## Reskin verdict
**Major reflavor + small rules tweak.** Recommend path 1 or 2; cut as last resort. Anathema is asset, not liability. Wildsong should be renamed (suggestion: "Adamic tongue" or "Eden-speech").

## Watch-outs
- Animal Empathy: fine.
- Plant Empathy: fine (Edenic fit).
- Wild Shape / Untamed Form: trickier theologically — does a holy person turn into a bear? Probably restrict to Order-of-the-Leaf style permanent-companion options or specific saint legends (St. Francis preaching to birds, not BECOMING a bird).
- Storm Order's elemental tie blends well with Elijah/Moses miracle-types.
