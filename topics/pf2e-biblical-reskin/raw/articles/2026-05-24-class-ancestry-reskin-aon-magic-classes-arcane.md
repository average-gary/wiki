# Arcane / Occult Magic Classes — biblical-setting reskin notes

## Wizard (ID=39, Int-key, arcane prepared)
- Schools (Mentalism / Protean / Ars Grammatica / Battle / Civic / Boundary / Unified).
- Arcane Bond + Arcane Thesis + Drain Bonded Item.
- "Treats magic like a science, cross-referencing the latest texts."

**Reskin path**: this class is the cleanest mapping of the *magia* (lawful learned art) vs *goeteia* (forbidden necromantic art) distinction. Frame as Lewisian "loremaster" — natural-philosophy practitioner whose study is permitted because he engages with God's created order rather than commanding spirits. School of Ars Grammatica (Word-magic) is theologically lush: "In the beginning was the Word." Ban necromancy and conjuration-of-spirits schools as forbidden (as Augustine does in *De Doctrina Christiana* II).

## Sorcerer (ID=62, Cha-key, innate spontaneous)
- Bloodlines determine spell tradition.
- **Angelic** = divine tradition (descended from celestials) — direct biblical hook (Genesis 6 / Nephilim residue / Cornelius's "devout man and one that feared God").
- Demonic / Diabolic / Hag / Shadow / Undead = obvious cuts or villain-only.

**Reskin path**: SHIP THE ANGELIC BLOODLINE PROMINENTLY. Frame as "charism-bearer" — spiritual gift (1 Cor 12) inherited via faithful lineage. The "born with magic in your blood" narrative is awkward theologically (election by birth?), but is recoverable as election-language (Jeremiah 1:5 "before I formed thee in the belly I knew thee"). Imperial / Wyrmblessed / Phoenix / Elemental survivable as creation-aligned. Aberrant / Demonic / Diabolic / Hag / Nymph / Psychopomp / Shadow / Undead = cut or NPC-villain.

## Witch (ID=38, Int-key, prepared via familiar)
- Patron is "mysterious supernatural entity"; communicates in "abstract signs such as omens, dreams."
- Familiar is conduit; "patron does not take kindly to repeated requests for aid."

**Reskin path**: this is the hardest because the rules are explicit that the patron is mysterious-and-not-fully-known. In a Christian setting that is the definition of dealing-with-spirits-of-unknown-allegiance (Deuteronomy 18:10-12). Three options: (a) **cut entirely**, (b) **villain-only NPC class**, (c) **redeem narrowly** as a vowed servant of a specific guardian angel or saint, retag patron from "mysterious" to "named celestial intercessor." Recommend (a) or (b); (c) is fragile.

## Magus (ID=17, Str/Dex-key, arcane prepared + Spellstrike)
- Hybrid Studies; Arcane Cascade; Conflux focus spells.
- Same theological problem as Wizard, plus the gish action-economy.

**Reskin path**: same as Wizard — frame as warrior-loremaster / Templar-scholar. Spellstrike reads as "anointed strike." Sparkling Targe study reads as faith-shield. Ship if Wizard ships.

## Psychic (ID=68, Int/Cha-key, occult innate)
- Subconscious + Conscious Mind; Psi cantrips; Unleash Psyche.
- Pure mind-magic.

**Reskin path**: hardest of all. "Mind-magic" is the closest 5e/PF2e analogue to NEW Age psi-phenomena, which Christian frameworks treat skeptically at best. Two options: (a) **cut**, (b) **radically reframe as gifts of discernment** (1 Cor 12 word-of-knowledge, word-of-wisdom) — but this collapses the mechanical identity. Recommend cut.

## Verdict tier (arcane block)
- Wizard: reflavor (loremaster). KEEP with school restrictions.
- Sorcerer: reflavor + bloodline cuts. KEEP (esp. Angelic).
- Magus: reflavor (templar-scholar). KEEP if Wizard kept.
- Witch: cut or villain-only.
- Psychic: cut.
