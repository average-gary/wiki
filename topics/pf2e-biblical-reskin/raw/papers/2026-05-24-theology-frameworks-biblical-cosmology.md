# Biblical Cosmology

## Three-Tier Universe (Old Testament base layer)
- **Shamayim** (heavens) — above the firmament.
- **Eres** (earth) — flat disk, the human realm.
- **Sheol** — underworld, beneath the earth.

This Mesopotamian-style cosmology dominates the OT until Hellenistic re-cosmologizing (post-330 BCE).

## The Firmament (raqia')
- Solid dome (Genesis 1:6-8) separating "waters above" from "waters below."
- "Windows of heaven" open during the Flood (Gen 7:11).
- Sun, moon, and stars are set IN the firmament (Gen 1:14-17).

## Multiple Heavens
- Second-temple Judaism develops 3-, 7-, and 10-heaven schemes (1 Enoch, 2 Enoch, Testament of Levi).
- **Paul's "third heaven" / Paradise** (2 Cor 12:2-4) reflects this.
- Christian tradition typically settles on three: atmospheric (clouds/birds), celestial (sun/moon/stars), and the divine throne-room.

## The Throne Room (Revelation 4-5)
- God enthroned, sea of glass, four living creatures (cherubim/seraphim conflation), 24 elders, the Lamb.
- Echoes Isaiah 6 (seraphim) and Ezekiel 1 (cherubim, wheels-within-wheels = thrones/ophanim).
- This is the "highest heaven" — the worship-court, not just a location but the seat of cosmic authority.

## The Underworld — Stratification
- **Sheol/Hades** — general dead (OT and early NT).
- **Gehenna** — fiery final punishment (Valley of Hinnom imagery).
- **Tartarus** — deepest pit, prison for fallen angels (2 Pet 2:4; in Greek myth, lower than Hades).
- **The Abyss / Bottomless Pit** (Greek *abyssos*) — primordial chaos, Rev 9:1-2 (locust-demons), Rev 20:1-3 (Satan bound 1000 years). Distinct from Gehenna; functions as a holding cell.
- **Lake of Fire** (Rev 20:14-15) — final destination after the last judgment; even Death and Hades are cast in.

## The Abyss / Tehom
- *Tehom* (Hebrew "deep") — primordial waters of Gen 1:2.
- Rev 13:1 — the beast rises "from the sea." Sea = chaos.
- "No more sea" in Rev 21:1 = chaos abolished.

## The New Jerusalem (Rev 21-22)
- City descends from heaven to a renewed earth — heaven and earth are reunited, not separated.
- Cubic dimensions (12,000 stadia per side) echo the Holy of Holies.
- River of life, tree of life — Eden restored and amplified.
- No temple — God himself is the temple.
- No sun — God's glory is the light.

## Worldbuilding implication
Biblical cosmology has **two end-states**: the present three-tier cosmos (heavens / earth / underworld with the abyss as cosmic prison), and the post-eschaton **renewed unified cosmos** where heaven descends to earth. A campaign can be set before, during, or after the eschatological transition — each gives radically different metaphysics.
