# WDVA Federal Cases Originating in FrCo / Winchester (2022-2026)

Frederick County and Winchester sit in the **U.S. Attorney's Office Western District of Virginia (WDVA)**. Cases route through the **Harrisonburg division** (drug/gun) and **Charlottesville division** (threats/explosives grand juries).

## Investigation source pattern

- **NWVRDGTF (Northwest Virginia Regional Drug & Gang Task Force)** is the **dominant local feeder for federal drug indictments** out of Frederick County / Winchester / Stephens City. Joint posture with DEA, ATF, VSP.
- **FBI** carries threats / explosives cases (Carillo, Drake) and the McDonald public-corruption case alongside IRS-CI.
- **ICE/HSI** drives the 2026 federal-felony immigration arrest volume.

## Notable cases (10 surfaced)

### 1. Hector Espinosa — drug + firearms (gang) — Sentenced Nov 19, 2025

- **Defendant**: Stephens City, VA, age 37; Sureño 13 gang affiliate
- **Charges**: 21 USC 841/846 conspiracy to distribute methamphetamine; 18 USC 922(g)(1) felon in possession
- **Conduct**: Central role in moving meth through Northern Shenandoah Valley. Controlled buy Feb 21, 2023 → co-defendants Ruiz-Gonzalez and Vicencio. Answered search warrant with firearm in waistband; threatened a witness.
- **Investigation**: DEA, ATF, VSP, **NWVRDGTF**
- **Outcome**: **240 months (20 years)** federal prison
- **Sources**: justice.gov/usao-wdva/pr/gang-member-sentenced-20-years-federal-prison-drug-and-gun-charges; Winchester Gazette; WVVA

### 2. Frank Lucio Carillo — threats against public officials — Sentenced May 14, 2026

- **Defendant**: Winchester, VA, age 66
- **Charges**: 18 USC 871 / 875(c) — threats to kill, kidnap, or injure VP Kamala Harris and former President Barack Obama (14 counts)
- **Conduct**: Posted thousands of threatening messages on Gettr under "joemadarats1" from Winchester residence
- **Investigation**: FBI; weapons seized at arrest (Aug 2024)
- **Outcome**: **30 months** federal prison
- **Source**: justice.gov/usao-wdva/pr/virginia-man-indicted-making-threats-against-public-officials

### 3. Gerald Leonard Drake — domestic terrorism / explosives — Sentenced Aug 2023

- **Defendant**: Winchester, VA, age 63; Civil War reenactor
- **Charges**: 18 USC 844(d) mailing threatening communications; 18 USC 2261A stalking; 26 USC 5861 unregistered destructive device
- **Conduct**: Planted **pipe bomb at Cedar Creek Battlefield** (Middletown, FrCo/Shenandoah county line) during Oct 2017 reenactment; sent letters posing as "Antifa" to Cedar Creek Battlefield Foundation president and Winchester Star → cancellation of annual reenactment
- **Investigation**: FBI, ATF
- **Outcome**: **9 years** federal prison

### 4. Kenneth James Hughes, Jr. — fentanyl distribution resulting in death — Sentenced Aug 2023

- **Defendant**: Winchester, VA, age 27
- **Charges**: 21 USC 841 — distribution of fentanyl (pills disguised as oxycodone)
- **Conduct**: Facilitated sale of fatal dose of fentanyl in Oct 2020
- **Investigation**: **NWVRDGTF**, DEA
- **Outcome**: **84 months** federal prison
- **PACER**: 5:21-cr-00008 (vawd, Harrisonburg)

### 5. Lamont Antoine Parson — synthetic drug distribution — Sentenced Jun 27, 2022

- **Defendant**: Winchester, VA, age 40
- **Charges**: 21 USC 841/846 conspiracy to distribute eutylone (sold as "Molly"/"Ecstasy")
- **Conduct**: Multiple controlled buys starting Jan 2020
- **Investigation**: **NWVRDGTF** (lead)
- **Outcome**: **72 months** federal prison

### 6. Jennifer Rae McDonald — public-corruption / wire fraud — Sentenced May 29, 2024

- **Defendant**: Front Royal, VA (Warren County, immediately south of FrCo), age 45
- **Charges**: 7 wire fraud + 6 bank fraud + 16 money laundering + 1 aggravated identity theft
- **Conduct**: As Executive Director of Front Royal-Warren County EDA, **diverted >$5.2 million in public funds** for personal real estate and gambling
- **Investigation**: FBI, IRS-CI; prosecuted by USAO-WDVA (US Attorney Christopher R. Kavanaugh)
- **Outcome**: Convicted Nov 2023 after 9-week jury trial; **14 years** federal prison

> Falls just outside FrCo proper (Front Royal is in Warren) but the headline I-81-corridor / Northern Shenandoah federal fraud case of the period.
