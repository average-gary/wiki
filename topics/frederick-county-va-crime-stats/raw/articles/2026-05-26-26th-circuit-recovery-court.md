# 26th Judicial Circuit Specialty Dockets

**Verdict (CORRECTION TO EARLIER WIKI)**: The 26th Judicial Circuit (Frederick + Winchester + Clarke + Warren + Shenandoah + Page) **DOES** operate Recovery Courts and a Behavioral Health Docket. Earlier wiki framing said it might not — that was wrong.

## Programs operating

| Program | Service area | Type | Eligibility |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Northwest Regional Adult Recovery Court (NRARC)** | Winchester + Frederick + Clarke | Adult felony recovery | Non-violent felony charge; Commonwealth's Attorney approval |
| **Warren County Adult Recovery Court (WCARC)** | Warren County + Town of Front Royal | Adult felony recovery | Non-violent felony |
| **Shenandoah County Adult Recovery Court (SCARC)** | Shenandoah County | Adult felony recovery | Non-violent felony |
| **Northwest Regional Adult Behavioral Health Docket (NRABHD)** | Winchester + Frederick + Clarke | Misdemeanor mental-health docket | Misdemeanor charge + serious mental-illness diagnosis |
| ~~Veterans Treatment Docket~~ | — | **Not operating** | n/a |

## NRARC (the FrCo + Winchester drug court)

- **Established July 2016** — described as "Virginia's 26th of its kind" at 7-year anniversary
- Advisory committee formed April 2015
- NSVSUC (Northern Shenandoah Valley Substance Use Coalition) incorporated May 2015 to operate the program
- **Eligibility**: non-violent felony + Commonwealth's Attorney sign-off
- Applies to Winchester city + Frederick County + Clarke County

## Page County coverage

**Page County is in the 26th Circuit but not currently included** in any specialty-docket service area. Possible referral pathway through Warren or Shenandoah dockets, unconfirmed.

## NSVSUC operator

The **Northern Shenandoah Valley Substance Use Coalition** (NSVSUC) administers the recovery courts in partnership with the courts.

**Funding sources**:
- HIDTA AIMS Treatment/Prevention Grant (the WB-HIDTA federal grant pipeline)
- Bureau of Justice Assistance (DOJ) grant
- Supreme Court of Virginia grants
- Direct allocations from: City of Winchester, Frederick County, Clarke County, Warren County, Town of Front Royal, Shenandoah County, Valley Health
- 2016 launch: federal grant covering coordinator + intensive outpatient treatment + team training

## Statewide context

Per *Winchester Star* (undated): NRARC was "one of 39 adult drug treatment courts in Virginia, with 10 added in the last three years in response to the opiate epidemic."

## Behavioral Health Docket (NRABHD)

Now operational — expansion the NSVSUC pushed for after NRARC's success.

Per *Winchester Star* article *"Success of region's drug court prompts request for mental health court"*: the coalition's request was approved.
