# Frederick County, Virginia — Crime Statistics

Compilation of crime statistics for **Frederick County, Virginia** (county seat: Winchester, in the Northern Shenandoah Valley) and adjacent jurisdictions. Sources: FBI UCR/NIBRS, Virginia State Police *Crime in Virginia* annual reports, Frederick County Sheriff's Office (FCSO) public records, Winchester Police Department reports, and U.S. Census demographic data for normalization.

## Scope

- **Geographic**: Frederick County (the county itself, ~96,000 population), the **independent city of Winchester** (separate jurisdiction surrounded by FrCo, ~28,000 pop), and adjacent context (Clarke, Shenandoah, Warren counties).
- **Time window**: 5-10 year trends where available; latest year cited.
- **Categories**: violent crime (homicide, robbery, aggravated assault, rape), property crime (burglary, larceny, MV theft, arson), drug offenses, DUI, weapons offenses, immigration-related arrests, juvenile arrests, clearance rates.
- **Jurisdictional breakdown**: FCSO vs Winchester PD vs VSP vs federal (DEA/FBI/ICE) where surfaced.
