## Why it matters

Mender has historically targeted Yocto/Linux fleets (think Raspberry Pi-class). Extending to MCU class implies the underlying signed-envelope + device-key model scales down to constrained hardware — without abandoning the (identity attributes + rotatable keypair) split.
