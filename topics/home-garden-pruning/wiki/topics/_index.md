# Topics

Per-plant pruning playbooks.

- [[cherry-pruning|Cherry pruning — Kristin and BlackGold]] — late-July post-harvest pruning is the dominant rule; bacterial canker; training systems
- [[blackberry-pruning|Thornless blackberry pruning]] — biennial cycle; summer tip primocanes; remove spent floricanes; lateral pruning in winter
- [[rose-pruning|Shrub and Knock Out rose pruning]] — late-winter timing; one-third renewal annual or hard reset every 2-3 years; RRD sanitation
- [[raspberry-pruning|Raspberry pruning]] — same biology as blackberry but species-specific (red summer-bearing, fall-bearing mow-down, black/purple summer tip)
- [[blueberry-pruning|Blueberry pruning]] — cane renewal cycle; ~20%/year; flower-bud removal years 1-2; rabbiteye vs highbush
