# Home Garden Pruning

Topic wiki investigating pruning theory and technique for five plant categories the user maintains:

- **Sweet cherries** — `Kristin` and `BlackGold` cultivars (semi-dwarf to standard)
- **Thornless blackberries** (semi-erect/erect cultivars: Triple Crown, Navaho, Apache, Chester)
- **Shrub roses** — Knock Out and similar landscape shrub roses
- **Raspberries** — summer-bearing and fall-bearing (primocane vs floricane)
- **Blueberries** — highbush and rabbiteye

## Top-level questions
1. WHEN to prune each plant (dormant vs summer; late winter vs after harvest)?
2. WHAT to cut (1-yr vs 2-yr wood; primocane vs floricane; suckers; water sprouts; old canes)?
3. HOW MUCH (renewal ratio; thinning vs heading; light vs hard cuts)?
4. HOW (cut placement, angle, distance from bud; tools; sanitation between plants)?
5. TRAINING systems (central leader, modified central leader, open center for cherries; T-trellis or 4-arm Kniffin for brambles)?
6. DISEASE risk windows (silver leaf, bacterial canker on cherries; cane blight on brambles; rose rosette/black spot)?
