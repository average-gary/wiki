# Grammarly — Common AI words and phrases

**Quality: 3/5.** Commercial vendor content, but from an established writing-tools company; strongest for the phrase-and-structure angle plus reviewer advice.

## Enumerated words / phrases

- **High-frequency words:** delve into, underscore, pivotal, realm, harness, illuminate, shed light on, facilitate, refine, bolster, differentiate, streamline.
- **Buzzwords:** revolutionize, innovative, cutting-edge, game-changing, transformative, "seamless integration", "scalable solution".
- **Transition/structured phrases:** "that being said", "at its core", "to put it simply", "this underscores the importance of", "a key takeaway is", "from a broader perspective".
- **Hedging/qualifiers:** "generally speaking", "typically", "tends to", "arguably", "to some extent", "broadly speaking".

## Structural / stylistic tells

- "A formal, robotic tone" with repeated sentence structures.
- "Repetitive phrasing" and reliance on "familiar phrases, clichés".
- "Predictable formatting" — "title-case headings, neatly formatted bullet points".
- Heavy use of "hedging phrases—words that soften statements".

## Reviewer / editor advice

- "Always review and refine AI-generated text to ensure it aligns with your intent."
- "Add personal insights or anecdotes"; "Trade formulaic structures for something more surprising or original."
- "Use AI to enhance, not replace, creativity"; keep "human oversight at the center."

Translates the tells into phrase-level and structural cues an everyday editor uses, plus actionable "how to fix" guidance. Complements the corpus word lists with the canned-phrase layer.
