# Bevy Foundation

Washington-state nonprofit, **501(c)(3) public charity**. Incorporated 2024-03-11, charity status granted 2024-09-25 ([[bevy-foundation-page.md|foundation page]]). Donations from US taxpayers are tax-deductible.

## Why it exists

[[bevy-fourth-birthday.md|Fourth birthday post (2024-08)]] articulates the rationale: pre-Foundation, founder Carter Anderson held all IP, domains, and infrastructure personally — "no longer sustainable." The Foundation:

- Centralizes funding (donations now go to the org, not to individuals)
- Provides organizational legitimacy for company partnerships and grant eligibility
- Lets maintainers be employees rather than freelancers
- Creates continuity that doesn't depend on any one person staying solvent

## Board

Five directors ([[bevy-foundation-page.md|foundation page]]):

- Carter Anderson — President / interim Treasurer
- Alice Cecile — Secretary
- François Mockers
- Robert Swain
- James Liu

Maintainers can be offered Director status by board vote; new directors are elected by sitting directors.

## Funding

- 2 full-time employees + 1 contractor as of [[bevy-fifth-birthday.md|August 2025]]
- Carter takes a 50%+ pay cut from market rate
- Foundation is "drastically under-funded for our ambitions" — Carter's own framing
- Public budget page lists target salary $150,000/yr for the full-time maintainer (Alice Cecile); ~90% of income → maintainer salary, ~10% buffer
- Actual revenue/donation totals are not on the public budget page
