# TWIB 2024-06-03

## Key findings

- Tiny Glade (Pounce Light) shipped its Steam demo built on Bevy ECS plus custom renderer.
- Pounce Light gave a public talk on their rendering technology — confirms Bevy is used as foundation but heavily customized.
- Around the same window: deferred-renderer SSR via raymarching (Tomasz Stachowiak, a Tiny Glade co-creator), bevy_light_2d (general 2D lighting plugin), bevy_hanabi 0.11 (GPU particles with trails/ribbons), bevy_ratatui (terminal UI) — production-adjacent ecosystem.
- DrLuke ran a live 2-hour Bevy-based VJ performance at GulaschProgrammierNacht — non-game applied use.
