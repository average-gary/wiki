# Bevy Foundation governance page

## Key findings

- Washington-state non-profit, **501(c)(3) public charity** (granted 2024-09-25).
- Incorporated 2024-03-11.
- **Board**: Carter Anderson (President / interim Treasurer), Alice Cecile (Secretary), François Mockers, Robert Swain, James Liu.
- Maintainers offered Director status by board vote; new directors elected by sitting directors.
- Mission: "promote, protect, and advance" the free-and-open-source Bevy Engine.
- Funding allocation: maintainer salaries, infrastructure (hosting, CI), nonprofit compliance overhead.
- Public transparency: bylaws, meeting minutes, incorporation docs, annual budgets all published.
