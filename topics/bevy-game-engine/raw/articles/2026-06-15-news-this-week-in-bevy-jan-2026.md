# TWIB January 2026 roundup

## Key findings

- **Bevy 0.18.0-rc.2** in release-candidate phase early January 2026; main branch entered **0.19 development cycle** by Jan 5.
- **Active organized efforts (working groups / SIG-equivalents) in late 2025 / early 2026**:
  - WESL-ification (shader language migration)
  - First-Party Tilemaps (shipped 0.17)
  - Better Release Notes
  - Decoupled Rendering
  - "Turtles all the way down" (`!Send` data in ECS)
  - Inspector General (entity inspector)
  - Async ECS ergonomics
  - Font ergonomics (shipped in 0.18)
- In-flight rendering work: lighting/fresnel/brightness fixes, parallel GPU buffer writes PR.
- Ecosystem: `bevy_tnua` (character controller) underwent major breaking refactor for 0.18; Exofactory, POLDERS, Lost in Time, Varrbound projects highlighted.
- Clarification of `IoTaskPool` vs `AsyncComputeTaskPool` distinction surfaced in community Q&A.
