# Bevy's Fourth Birthday (2024-08-10)

The Foundation pivot.

## Key findings

- **Bevy Foundation launched March 2024** — shifted governance from individual leadership (Cart held all IP/domains/infra) to a board, cited as "no longer sustainable."
- Foundation rationale: centralizes funding to avoid "popularity contest" donations, gives organizational legitimacy for company partnerships.
- Three releases in year 4: 0.12, 0.13, 0.14 — renderer matured to deferred rendering, virtual geometry, irradiance volumes, reflection probes, volumetric fog.
- **41% of all PRs in Bevy's history merged in year 4 alone** — Alice Cecile hired as full-time staff engineer accelerated velocity.
- Year-4 stats: 1,027 unique contributors (+39% YoY), 1.49M crates.io downloads.
- Hooks and Observers added on top of a now-stable ECS API; visual editor still unreleased after four years; Audio/UI/Assets SME slots unfilled.
