# TWIB 2025-06-30 — LongStory 2

## Key findings

- **LongStory 2** by Bloom Digital — sequel to the award-winning visual novel LongStory — shipped to Steam (app 2427820) and itch.io, built on Bevy.
- Adds a second commercial-shipped genre (visual novel / dating sim) to the Tiny Glade (cozy builder) data point.
- Multi-platform release (Steam + itch) demonstrates Bevy's cross-platform deployment story for a commercial studio.
- Concurrent ecosystem signal: BevyFeathers (UI widgets) and type-erased materials — the latter relevant for asset-pipeline-heavy production games.
