# GROB E-Motor Turnkey Lines

- GROB builds **fully automated turn-key EV traction-motor lines** and stand-alone/semi-automatic machines: stator winding → final e-axle assembly → EOL test.
- **Four winding technologies matched to volume/application:**
  - **Hairpin** — preformed rectangular open-coil segments, "highest filling ratios."
  - **Continuous Hairpin** — prefabricated wire mat inserted into stator; handles small cross-sections without complex circuitry.
  - **Insertion** — flyer winding + twist-free; consumer goods through automotive.
  - **Needle winding** — round wire, used for rotor production.
- "eAx" case: modular production line for hairpin stators, final assembly, laser welding, e-axle testing — aimed at rapid prototyping → high-volume scaling.
