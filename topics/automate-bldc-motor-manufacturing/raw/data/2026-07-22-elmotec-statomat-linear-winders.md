# Linear Winders (Elmotec-Statomat / Schaeffler)

- **Linear winding niche**: best for small components with high turn counts + small wire diameters, OR rotationally symmetric segments with very large wire diameters. Supports air coils, double segments, and flat wire.
- **Speed**: up to **1,000 rpm**; example **24 s cycle time** at 1,000 rpm for an e-mobility part. Controls the winding "belly" (bulge) at max rpm; monitors wire elongation + wire force for layer accuracy.
- **Segmented stators**: individually wound stator teeth / coil carriers, then automatically assembled — the key enabler for high fill factor + compact design in EV hybrid modules and servo motors (power steering, machine-tool drives, robots).
- **Automation add-ons**: integrated wire-end **stripping unit** and integrated **joining station** for assembling segments; optimized set-up for tool changeover to handle high type variety.
- Elmotec-Statomat (owned by Schaeffler) is also known for continuous-hairpin / wave-winding stator tech.
