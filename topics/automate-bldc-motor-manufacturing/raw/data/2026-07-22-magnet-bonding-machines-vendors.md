# Automated Magnet Bonding & Magnetizing (vendor evidence)

**Rimac Machines (magnet bonding):**
- Handles **both magnetized and non-magnetized magnets**; flags that magnetized magnets present **crushing/safety risks to operators** — the core reason unmagnetized (post-magnetization) flow is preferred.
- Covers **both SPM (surface bonding) and IPM (slot insertion)**; small actuators to large torque motors, flat + curved magnets.
- High-precision **adhesive dosing**; **polarity control** prevents orientation errors when pre-magnetized magnets are unavoidable. Companion line offers rotor banding (sleeve/band retention).

**MagnetAct (automatic magnetizing machine, CD type):**
- Input AC 380 V; charging voltage **1000–5000 V** (customizable); discharge energy up to **10,000 J (10 kJ)**; **cycle time 5–20 s/piece**.
- In-line robotic load/unload; integrated charge + magnetize + **flux test** in one station; optional barcode + data export. Custom fixtures for arc/block/ring/multipole; NdFeB/SmCo/ferrite.

**Magnetic Innovations (robotic SPM rotor cell, MI-F frameless, 110–485 mm rotors):**
- **Atmospheric plasma cleaning/activation** of magnet + rotor surfaces before adhesive (surface prep is the reliability driver).
- Adhesive bonding with controlled dosage; robot places each magnet.
- **Per-magnet magnetic property check** before placement, then **full magnetic-field scan** of the finished rotor stored as a serialized "rotor fingerprint."

**Supplementary vendors:** Magnetic Instrumentation CD magnetizers (100 J to >100 kJ; caps rated 10⁷ discharges — durability metric for high-volume in-line cycling); ACAM automated bonding cell (camera-guided pick, centering, Loctite dispensing, defined-force press, ~120 s/pc).
