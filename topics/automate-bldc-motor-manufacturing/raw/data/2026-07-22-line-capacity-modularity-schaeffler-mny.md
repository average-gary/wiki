# E-Drive Line Capacity & Modularity

**Schaeffler Special Machinery (e-axle line):**
- Integrated e-axle assembly + testing line designed for **~175,000 units/year** (gigafactory-scale anchor).
- **Modular, scalable**: workstations added/removed to match demand.
- Assembly spans **"manual workstations to fully automated high-speed machines"** — the manual → semi-auto → hard-automation continuum, explicit.
- Rotor platform supports **both PMSM and asynchronous** on one platform.
- **Cobot-based human-robot collaboration (HRC) with force feedback** for gear joining (high-mix flexibility). >1000 test machines fielded (leak, NVH, HV).

**MNY hairpin stator lines:**
- Capacity **200,000+ units/year** for passenger-EV stator lines.
- **Five-stage line**: slot insulation → hairpin coil forming (straighten/cut/bend/laser-strip) → coil insertion (evolved 2–4 → **6–8 layers in one operation**) → end flanging/twisting/welding → coating/varnishing/potting (VPI).
- Welding takt: TIG ~2 s/point (≥500 N pull-out), laser fast (≥300 N); IATF 16949.
- **Single-station changeover as low as 30 min**; multiple stator models on one line; AGV material handling under **MES control**; in-process monitoring + data collection at every station.
