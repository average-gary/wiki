# Laser Hairpin Welding & Stripping (Coherent)

- Insulation must be stripped from every pin end before welding. **Mechanical stripping removes copper, leaves a rough texture, causes gaps + part-fit-up problems between pins**, and tools wear → process inconsistency, frequent maintenance, unplanned downtime.
- **Porosity is the key weld defect**: polymer/insulation residue contains hydrogen + carbon that enter the molten pool and form porosities on solidification, degrading mechanical + electrical weld performance.
- Advocated solution: two-step laser cleaning (CO₂ bulk removal + UV solid-state precision cleaning) rather than single-wavelength.
