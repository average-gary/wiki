# AI EV Motor Winding Inspection (iFactory)

- **In-line machine-vision defect classes**: varnish cracks, conductor exposure, dielectric degradation, broken wires, improper solder joints, connection defects, improper conductor spacing — high-res cameras at "micron-level precision."
- **AI models** trained on 5M+ images; claimed **94% detection accuracy** vs 68% manual *(vendor claim — illustrative)*.
- **Magnet placement**: vision-guided fixtures verify position + polarity to sub-mm before rotor closeout; air-gap/concentricity checks flag eccentricity.
- **Line integration**: continuous surge + hi-pot during winding (before encapsulation seals faults in); **MES connectivity (SAP/Oracle/Siemens)**; defect detection triggers automated work orders + immediate holds; supplier defect scorecards; IATF 16949 audit-ready records.
- Philosophy: catch defects **in-process at the assembly station**, not only at EOL; predictive flagging of likely balance-test failures.
