# Kraken Funds Full-Time Bitcoin Rust Maintainer

Kraken announcement, 2022-02-08. Already linked from TWiR issue 428 (2022-02-09).

## Key facts
- **Maintainer**: Tobin Harding (`tcharding`), full-time on rust-bitcoin.
- **Fund**: Established Sept 2021 in memory of **Tamás Blummer**, an early Bitcoin Rust contributor at Kraken.
- **Advisors**: Andrew Poelstra (Blockstream Director of Research), Pierre Rochard, Tuur Demeester.
- **Significance**: First major institutional funding of rust-bitcoin maintenance.
