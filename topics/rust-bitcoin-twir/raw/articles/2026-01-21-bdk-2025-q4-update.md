# BDK 2025 Q4 Update

Quarterly recap from bitcoindevkit.org, 2026-01-21.

## Headline news
- **MetaMask shipped a self-custodial Bitcoin plugin built on `bdk-wasm`** (WebAssembly).
- **Bitcoin Safe deployed Kyoto** (compact block filters client) on mainnet.
- **`rust-cktap`** adopted in iOS app SatsBuddy for SATSCARD interactions.
- Long-standing Electrum client issues fixed; new "Release Guide" + "Library Tiers" docs.
- Four BDK talks at TABConf (Merkle trees, transaction complexity, docs, silent payments).

## Crates / versions
- `bdk_wallet 2.2.0` & `2.3.0`
- `bdk-ffi 2.2.0`
- `rust-electrum-client 0.24.1`
