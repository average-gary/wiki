# Sjors Provoost — explicit reverse-translator recruiting

## The quote

> "currently seeking contributions related to SV2 (Stratum V2), template provider functionality, and **reverse-translator development**."

— from the bio of Sjors Provoost (https://github.com/Sjors), a Bitcoin Core / SV2 contributor.

## Why this matters

- This is the only public, named, sourced reference to a "reverse translator" as a development priority.
- Sjors has a Bitcoin Core fork integrating SV2 template-provider functionality; his interest spans the full SV2 stack.
- Pairs with **sv2-spec issue #102** (which names the concept canonically): the spec mentions it, the contributor recruits for it.

## Implication

Demand exists from inside the SRI ecosystem, not just outside it. The reverse translator is not a third-party patch — it's a structural gap that SRI contributors are aware of and trying to solicit help for. This shifts the build-vs-defer calculus: implementing it is upstream-aligned work, not a fork.
