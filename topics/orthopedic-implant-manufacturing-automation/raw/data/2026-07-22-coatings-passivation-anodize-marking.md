# Coatings, Passivation, Anodizing & UDI Marking

**HA hydroxyapatite plasma-spray (HIMED MATRIX):**
- Thickness tiers: thin 5–15 µm, nominal 20–40 µm, thick 35–70 µm. **≥90% HA; crystallinity >62% per FDA guidance** (validated by quantitative XRD, ASTM F2024).
- Applied via **atmospheric plasma spray (APS) with a 6-axis ABB robot** for reproducible coverage of complex surfaces. Grit-blast pretreatment with resorbable "MCD apatitic" media, residue-free after ASTM F86 passivation.
- Mech qual: ASTM F1147 (tensile), F1044 (shear); conforms ASTM F1185, ISO 13779-2. On pedicle/SI screws, spinal plates, femoral stems, acetabular cups.
- **Porous coatings (Orchid / Lincotek):** Titanium Plasma Spray (TPS, CP-Ti), Asymmatrix ultra-rough high-friction, Fortify TPS (high porosity+bond for knees), thin TPS for Ti/PEEK spinal cages; Lincotek APS-Ti, VPS-Ti "TiGrowth" (higher purity), HA "Osprovit" — variable porosity/thickness/roughness on Ti/CoCr/stainless/PEEK.

**Passivation (Advanced Plating Tech — validated automated line):**
- **ASTM F86:** 20–45 v% nitric (SG 1.1197–1.285), RT ≥30 min, or 120–140 °F ≥20 min. **ASTM A967:** nitric 20–55 v% at 70–140 °F/20–30 min; **citric 4–10 w% at 70–160 °F/4–20 min**, pH 1.8–2.2. **ASTM A380** descaling.
- Removes free iron/embedded contaminants, builds passive oxide. Dedicated line: multi-stage ultrasonic clean, neutralization, multi-stage hot-DI ultrasonic rinse (final to **20 MΩ·cm**), drying oven. ISO 13485, IQ/OQ/PQ — a **validated automated batch-tank line**, not manual.

**Anodizing (DANCO — Type II per AMS 2488):**
- Anti-galling/wear; **fatigue strength up +20%**; no hydrogen embrittlement; no dimensional change (penetrating oxide); **>50% surface-finish improvement**.
- **Color by oxide-film thickness (voltage-controlled), not dye** — for part identification; re-coatable without stripping. Biocompatible, on CP-Ti and most Ti alloys.

**UDI laser marking (TRUMPF):**
- **TruMicro Mark 2000 ultrashort-pulse laser** → durable, corrosion-resistant, high-contrast, **passivation-resistant** marks surviving repeated cleaning. **2D Data Matrix + human-readable text** per FDA UDI; TRUMPF is a **GS1-accredited issuing agency**.
- Automation: **VisionLine Detect/Model** (part+position recognition), **VisionLine OCT/Code** (post-mark verification/OCR), **TruTops Mark** (database-driven UDI sequencing).
