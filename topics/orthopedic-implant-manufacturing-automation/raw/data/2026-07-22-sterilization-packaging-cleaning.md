# Back-End Automation — Sterilize / Package / Clean

**Sterilization methods (LSO):**
- **EtO (ISO 11135):** most widely used — **>50% of disposable devices**; ideal for temp-sensitive plastics/electronics; long aeration to purge residuals; can be in-house.
- **Gamma (ISO 11137):** optimal for sealed products, **implants**, high-density items needing deep penetration; can degrade some polymers over time; **outsourced** to contract irradiation.
- **E-Beam (ISO 11137):** low-density/thin-packaging, less polymer degradation, **faster than gamma** but less penetrating; **outsourced**.
- **Steam/autoclave (ISO 17665):** reusable metal instruments/heat-stable devices, **3–15 min cycles**; not for heat/moisture-sensitive plastics. **VHP (ISO 22441):** low-temp, limited lumen penetration.
- Cycle-time hierarchy: **e-beam = seconds, gamma = hours, EtO = hours-to-days**. Sterilization is a **batch/outsourced step**; validated to **SAL 10⁻⁶** (from process-chain sources: gamma 25–40 kGy).

**Automated packaging (Medicopax / Nelipak):**
- **HFFS (horizontal form-fill-seal)** is the automated route for sterile devices: unwinds film → thermoforms bottom cavity → loads product → seals top web in cleanroom; **hundreds of units/minute**.
- Sterile-barrier material by sterilization method: **Tyvek/PE = EtO**; **PET/PE or PET/PP transparent = gamma/e-beam**; nylon/PE puncture-resistant for sharps; foil for moisture/light-sensitive.
- Cleanroom: **ISO Class 7 minimum** for sterile packaging fill; fill zone often **ISO Class 5**. **ISO 11607-2 validation**: seal params vs worst-case film-lot variability, seal strength **ASTM F88**, integrity **ASTM F1929/F2096**, post-sterilization integrity studies. Nelipak tray/blister sealers Class-7-rated, RFID recipe matching, IQ/OQ/PQ.

**Cleaning vendors** (from vendor sub-question): UCM (multi-stage aqueous ultrasonic → ultrapure-water rinse → warm-air/vacuum/IR dry + passivation, cleanroom-compatible), Zenith Ultrasonics, FISA. Automated ultrasonic lines are highly automatable; the gate is **cleaning validation per lot** and blind holes/lumens.
