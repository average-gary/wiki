# Serial Metal-AM at Ortho OEMs

**Stryker — AMagine platform / Tritanium porous titanium:**
- Proprietary powder-bed-fusion branded **AMagine**; **Tritanium** = randomized 100–700 µm pores (mean 400–500 µm), **55–65% porosity**, "randomized yet reproducible" to mimic cancellous bone.
- Products: Monterey AL / Tritanium PL / Tritanium C spinal cages; knee tibial baseplate + metal-backed patella; hip. Early investor in **Concept Laser (L-PBF) + Arcam (EBM)** (both now GE Additive).
- **>300,000 Tritanium devices implanted over 10 years.** Cork, Ireland (Anngrove) facility 156,000 sq ft, ~600 jobs; ~$400M invested in AMagine (2016) + $200M Cork expansion (2019).

**Lima Corporate (now Enovis) — Trabecular Titanium via EBM:**
- **15 Arcam EBM machines** for mass production of hip cups (concrete installed count). First Delta TT acetabular cup CE-marked 2007. "Honeycomb cell" trabecular architecture; **~100,000 patients** treated; expanded to shoulder + partial knee (with Materialise). Cleared EU/US/Japan/Australia/China.

**Orchid Orthopedic — Arcam Spectra L (contract-manufacturing scale-out):**
- Fleet of **Arcam EBM Spectra L** (largest EBM build volume) for serial knee components + acetabular cups (installs from 2022, production ~2024). **"EB-LPB" feature tightly stacks parts** without quality loss (the stackable-cups mechanism). Package: **AP&C powders + Powder Recovery Station (PRS 30)** (automated powder handling). IoT + machine-health monitoring. Spectra L: Ø350×H430 mm, Ti6Al4V Grade 5 & 23, 4.5 kW beam, Point Melt.

**AddUp — FormUp 350 L-PBF (serial-production economics):**
- 4-laser, 30 µm layer, support-reduced builds. **21,735 hip cups/year per machine at 78% OEE** (rare published productivity/utilization figure). **152 spinal implants per build.** Density >99.9% (cups), 99.95% (spinal); ±0.05 mm on lattices; patient-specific 7–10 days; FDA 510(k) cleared parts.

**Also:** EOS **500+ M290 medical systems worldwide** (Ti64 ELI + CoCr); restor3d (custom-at-scale, Durham NC). In-situ monitoring: **EOSTATE Exposure OT** (optical tomography, 100% melt visibility) + **Smart Fusion** (closed-loop laser power → support-reduced designs; the "validation by monitoring instead of CT" model).
