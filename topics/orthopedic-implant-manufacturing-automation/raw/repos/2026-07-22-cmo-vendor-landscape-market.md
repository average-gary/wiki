# CMO / Vendor Landscape + Market

**Structural fact — consolidation:** **Tecomet + Orchid merger completed May 14, 2026**, operating as **Tecomet** (HQ Woburn MA + Mason MI). Scale: Tecomet **14 sites / 5 countries** + Orchid **10 facilities** (US, UK, Switzerland). Combined capability: forging, casting, precision machining, photochemical etching, finishing, **additive manufacturing**, **bone-ingrowth coatings**, **sterile packaging**. Products: joint recon, hips, knees, spine, trauma, extremities, dental. Explicit rationale: scale to invest in "advanced manufacturing, **automation**, operational excellence."

**Market sizing (GM Insights):** **$7B (2023) → $15.3B (2032), 8.8% CAGR.** Implants lead → $9.3B by 2032; forging/casting leads categories → $4.9B by 2032; Class II = 54.4% (2023). Primary driver = **outsourcing** (OEMs offload to CMOs); patient-specific/AM secondary.

**CMO roster:** ARCH Medical Solutions, Autocam Medical, Avalign Technologies, CRETEX Medical, LISI Medical, Norman Noble, Orchid, Paragon Medical (part of **Marmon / Berkshire Hathaway**), Tecomet, Viant, Integer Holdings.

**Machine-tool builders (medical/ortho):** **DMG MORI** (DMU 60 eVo 5-axis, CoCr knee at 40k rpm, Ra 0.8–0.3 µm; MATRIS/PH Cell/AMR automation), **Mazak**, **Willemin-Macodel** (Series 30–80, hip/plate/screw/cage/knee/dental; robots + cobots), **Tornos** (SwissDECO/EvoDECO/SwissNano; Ti/PEEK/CoCr/SS; RoboBar), **Star**, **Citizen Cincom**, **Matsuura** (MAM72 — 72 h unmanned, up to 32-pallet towers, 330–530-tool magazines).

**Robotic finishing:** **Acme Manufacturing** — **FANUC** authorized integrator, **>150 turnkey robotic finishing systems installed for orthopedics** (most of any vendor); programmable force compliance; knee/hip/plate/acetabular/spine/blades; CoCr, zirconium, Ti in cast/forged/MIM/machined. Also **AV&R** (75% cycle-time cut, ±15 µm removal consistency).

**Metal-AM OEMs:** EOS, Nikon SLM, GE Additive/Arcam (Colibrium), Renishaw, 3D Systems, AddUp.
